## Unlocking the Climbing Claws (Climb Master)

The first major milestone in your journey for **upgrades pilgrammed** veterans recommend is the Climbing Claws. This skill functions similarly to the Climbing Claws item but exists as a permanent movement upgrade, allowing you to scale vertical surfaces with significantly less stamina consumption.

To find the Duke of Rocks, head to the Mountain biome. Locate the central cabin and head to the right. You will likely encounter the Ice King in this vicinity. Since his 2026 rework, the Ice King is a mandatory stop for those needing Soul Fragments, which are often required for secondary gear upgrades. Once you bypass or defeat the Ice King, continue along the cliffside to find the Duke of Rocks. Simply speaking with him will grant you the skill, provided you meet the hidden progression requirements.

### Movement Synergy with Climbing Claws
*   **Triple Dash:** When combined with the Dash Master, you can perform up to three consecutive dashes while clinging to a wall.
*   **Stamina Management:** The upgrade reduces the "slip" penalty on icy surfaces, making Mountain traversal much safer.

## Mastering the Dash (Dash Master)

The Dash Master upgrade is obtained from Garth, located in the Elite Brax region. While the exact level requirements can fluctuate based on your current world tier, most players find that Garth is willing to teach the skill after you have proven your mettle against local elites. 

The Dash Master is arguably the most important of all **upgrades pilgrammed** offers because it provides i-frames (invincibility frames) during the animation. In the high-damage environment of 2026, being able to dash through a boss's projectile rather than jumping over it is a vital survival tactic.

## The Cloud City Challenge: Scurry Tech & Jump Master

The most complex movement upgrade to acquire is the "Jump Master" or Scurry Tech, handled by the NPC Adagio in Cloud City. Located near the slingshot transport system, Adagio requires a set of rare materials before he will upgrade your boots.

### Required Materials for Adagio
To complete this quest, you must gather the following items:

1.  **20 World Rubber:** Generally found as drops from mechanical enemies or looted from chests in the industrial sectors.
2.  **Cloud Boots:** A base item that must be in your inventory for the upgrade to trigger.
3.  **Great Red Bird Feather:** The rarest component, requiring a specific platforming challenge.

| Material | Quantity | Best Farming Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **World Rubber** | 20 | Industrial Zone / Cloud City Chests |
| **Cloud Boots** | 1 | Cloud City Vendor or Rare Drop |
| **Red Bird Feather** | 1 | The High Peaks (Above Cloud City) |

### How to Get the Great Red Bird Feather
To reach the Great Red Bird, you must travel to the isolated floating islands above Cloud City. This area is infested with **Tendrilings**, which can easily knock a player off the narrow platforms. 

Follow these steps for a successful retrieval:
1.  **Equip the Noxious Motor:** You will need the extra lift to reach the higher islands.
2.  **Use the Glider:** Hold out your glider while moving toward the **White Orbs**. These orbs act as mana-free propulsion points that launch you upward.
3.  **Avoid the Guardian:** A powerful elite bird protector patrols the final nest. While you can kill it, it is often faster to use **Sandy Boots** for a speed burst, pluck the feather, and immediately teleport or jump down.
4.  **Return to Adagio:** Once the feather is in your possession, speak to Adagio to finalize the Jump Master upgrade.

> **💡 Tip:** Using a **Sport Shawl** during this segment is highly recommended. The knockback resistance it provides will prevent Tendrilings from ruining your platforming run.

## Recommended Builds for Farming Upgrades

When hunting for **upgrades pilgrammed** players often struggle with the sheer density of mobs in the Mountain and Cloud City. A "cheese" build can significantly reduce the difficulty of these encounters.

One popular 2026 build involves the **Heavy Rusty Bow**. By focusing on high-impact ranged damage, you can pick off Tendrilings from a distance before they become a threat. Combine this with the **Noxious Motor** for vertical mobility and **Sandy Boots** for ground speed.

### Recommended Loadout
*   **Weapon:** Heavy Rusty Bow (for safe distance farming).
*   **Accessory 1:** Sport Shawl (knockback resistance).
*   **Accessory 2:** Noxious Motor (essential for the Red Bird climb).
*   **Boots:** Sandy Boots (temporary speed buffs).

## Advanced Movement Techniques

Once you have secured all the **upgrades pilgrammed** provides, you can begin utilizing "Prejunks" or advanced movement tech. This involves buffering a dash immediately after a jump while the Climbing Claws are active. This allows for a "super-jump" effect that can bypass large sections of the map's vertical obstacles.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I check if I have unlocked the upgrades?**

A: You can check your progress in the character menu. Look for the "Climb Master," "Dash Master," and "Jump Master" indicators. If they are highlighted, the **upgrades pilgrammed** NPCs have successfully applied the skills to your character.

**Q: Do I need to keep the Climbing Claws item in my inventory after getting the upgrade?**

A: No. Once the Duke of Rocks grants you the movement upgrade, it becomes a permanent part of your character's ability set. You can free up that inventory slot for more powerful combat accessories.

**Q: What is the fastest way to farm World Rubber in 2026?**

A: The most efficient method is farming the mechanical sentries in the outskirts of Cloud City. They have a high drop rate for World Rubber, and with a ranged build, you can clear them without taking significant damage.

**Q: Can I get the Great Red Bird Feather without the Noxious Motor?**

A: While technically possible using precise glider timing and the white orbs, it is extremely difficult. The Noxious Motor provides a safety net that makes the vertical climb significantly less frustrating.
