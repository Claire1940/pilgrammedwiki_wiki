## Understanding the Math: Why Certain Items Trade Higher

In 2026, the most successful participants in **pilgrammed trading** are those who understand "Pilgrim Math." The value of defensive items is determined by three distinct mechanics: Flat Defense, Damage Reduction (DR), and Mitigation. 

Items that offer **Mitigation** are often the most valuable. Unlike standard defense, mitigation acts as a multiplicative layer that is applied after all other calculations. For example, the **Blood Twin's Wicked Pull** provides 75% mitigation, effectively multiplying incoming damage by 0.25 after flat defense and DR have been subtracted. This makes it an S-tier trade asset.

### The Damage Formula Impact
The simplified formula used by the community to value gear is `m * t * (B - D)`, where:
*   **m** = Mitigation
*   **t** = Damage Taken (100% - Damage Reduction)
*   **B** = Base Enemy Damage
*   **D** = Flat Defense (0.5 damage reduction per 1 point of Defense)

Items that provide high Flat Defense are excellent for early to mid-game trading, but endgame **pilgrammed trading** focuses almost exclusively on sources of Mitigation and Damage Reduction.

## Dealing with Diminishing Returns

A common mistake in **pilgrammed trading** is overvaluing the stacking of Damage Reduction. DR in Pilgrammed suffers from depreciation based on the number of sources you have equipped. 

If you have a single source of 50% DR, you get the full 50%. However, adding a second source of 25% DR does not result in 75% total reduction. Instead, a quadratic equation is applied to the smaller value, often resulting in a total closer to 60%. 

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not trade for multiple high-DR items expecting them to stack linearly. The "Depreciation" mechanic means your third or fourth DR item provides significantly less value than the first.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the most valuable item in pilgrammed trading right now?**

A: Currently, items that provide unique "Mitigation" stacks, such as the Blood Twin or high-tier boss drops with multiplicative defense properties, hold the highest value. This is because they bypass the usual diminishing returns of standard Damage Reduction.

**Q: Why does my Damage Reduction not increase as much when I add a second item?**

A: Pilgrammed uses a depreciation formula for Damage Reduction. While the first source gives its full value, subsequent sources are reduced based on a quadratic equation. This prevents players from becoming 100% immortal through DR alone.

**Q: Is it better to trade for Flat Defense or Damage Reduction?**

A: It depends on the enemies you are fighting. Flat Defense is highly effective against enemies that deal many small hits (like Thieves), while Damage Reduction and Mitigation are essential for surviving high-damage boss attacks. In the **pilgrammed trading** market, Mitigation is generally considered more "valuable" for endgame content.

**Q: How do I calculate the value of a Cleric Hat?**

A: The value of a Cleric Hat is tied to its stacks. It can provide up to 10 Flat Defense and 15% Damage Reduction. Because it requires active combat to maintain these stats, it is valued as a high-tier "skill-based" defensive item rather than a passive buff.
