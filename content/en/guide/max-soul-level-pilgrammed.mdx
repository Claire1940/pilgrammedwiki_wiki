## Essential Skills for High-Level Traversal

Reaching the locations necessary for the **max soul level pilgrammed** grind often requires specific movement skills. Without these, you will find yourself unable to reach the high-altitude islands or the hidden depths where the best XP mobs spawn.

### The Wall Climb Technique
To obtain the climbing skill, you must visit the **Duke of Rocks**. He resides in the Mountain region and will task you with a quest to eliminate a specific number of mountain enemies. Once completed, you must scale to the very top of his mountain to turn in the quest.
*   **Effect:** Allows you to climb walls three times consecutively before needing to touch the ground.
*   **Why it matters:** Essential for reaching the Sky Islands and avoiding environmental hazards in the late game.

### The Dash Technique
Located at the **Elite Barracks**, you can find an NPC named **Garth**. While the exact requirements for his teaching can vary, it typically involves reaching a certain reputation with the Elite Knight Guild or defeating high-ranking skeletons.
*   **Effect:** Replaces your standard roll with an empowered dash.
*   **Why it matters:** This provides significantly better speed and iframe windows, which are mandatory for dodging the one-shot mechanics of level 80+ bosses.

### The Double Jump (Cloud Jump)
This is perhaps the most complex skill to acquire. You will need to purchase **Cloud Boots** from the shop vendor, then gather 20 Royal Rubber and a Great Redbird Feather. The feather is obtained by navigating a series of wind orbs in the far islands.

## Stat Allocation at Max Level

Once you reach the **max soul level pilgrammed** allows, your focus shifts from XP to build optimization. Depending on your preferred playstyle, you should dump your points into specific archetypes.

1.  **Strength Build:** Focuses on heavy weapons and high poise. Requires at least 60 points in Strength to wield late-game hammers.
2.  **Dexterity Build:** Focuses on katanas and fast-hitting weapons. High stamina is a secondary priority here.
3.  **Magic Build:** Requires a deep investment in Mana and Intelligence. This build is the fastest for clearing mobs but the glassiest against bosses.

## Hidden Mastery and Prejunks Content

In the 2026 "Prejunks" update, players who have hit the **max soul level pilgrammed** offers can access the Tangerine Islands. This area contains the highest-level enemies in the game. ## FAQ

**Q: What is the current max soul level in Pilgrammed for 2026?**

A: The current standard cap is level 100. However, players can continue to earn "Mastery" points for specific weapons even after hitting the maximum character level.

**Q: Does dying reset my progress toward the max soul level pilgrammed?**

A: No, you do not lose your total Soul Level upon death. However, you may lose some "unspent" souls if you are playing on a higher difficulty mode, so it is always wise to spend your points as soon as you level up.

**Q: Which skill is most important for leveling up?**

A: The Wall Climb skill from the Duke of Rocks is the most important early-to-mid-game skill. It allows you to access farming spots that are otherwise inaccessible, significantly speeding up your journey to the max level.

**Q: Can I reset my stats if I mess up my build?**

A: Yes, there is a specific NPC located in the Swamp region who will reset your stats in exchange for a rare item (usually a Rebirth Tear). This allows you to experiment with different builds once you reach the endgame.
