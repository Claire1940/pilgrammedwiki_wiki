## How to Obtain the Spark Scale Pilgrammed

To acquire the **spark scale pilgrammed**, you must hunt down the **Big Fish**. This boss is found within the oceanic regions of the map. Unlike land-based bosses, the Big Fish requires a boat and a bit of patience to locate and defeat effectively.

### Finding the Big Fish
The Big Fish typically spawns in deep water areas. Many players utilize whirlpools to teleport to random locations in the sea, which can sometimes place you directly in the vicinity of the boss. However, be cautious: fighting in the water is dangerous as you risk drowning if your boat is destroyed or if you fall out during the heat of combat.

### Combat Strategy: The "Cheese" Method
Because the Big Fish has high health and can be difficult to parry in the water, the most effective strategy in 2026 is "cheesing" it with ranged or magic weapons. 
1. **Stay on your boat:** Use a ranged weapon (like a bow or magic staff) to chip away at its health.
2. **Kite the boss:** Circle the fish constantly to avoid its lunges.
3. **Lure to Land:** If possible, lead the fish toward a shoreline or a shallow area where you can stand. This removes the risk of drowning and allows for more consistent parrying.

> **Warning:** If you fight the Big Fish in deep water without a stable platform, one mistake can lead to an instant death via drowning, regardless of your HP.

### Obtaining the Mana Sword
The Mana Sword is the primary damage dealer for this build. To craft it, visit the Friendly Witch in the Swamp. You will need:
*   **600 Gold**
*   **Mithril Sword:** Crafted at the Mountain Blacksmith using Mithril ore found in the mines.
*   **10 Arch Energy:** Obtained by defeating the Arch Mage. The Arch Mage is a difficult encounter, often requiring at least two kills to gather enough energy.

### Mastering the Shock Runner Boots
The Shock Runner Boots are what give this build its signature "lightning dash." To get them, you must defeat the **Shock Runner boss** in the Savannah.
*   **Summoning:** Buy a Copper Crystal from the shop NPC to summon him at any time.
*   **The Fight:** He is highly mobile, frequently teleporting and firing triple shots. You must stay aggressive and use parries to close the gap.

## Skill Tree and Stat Allocation

To maximize the effectiveness of the **spark scale pilgrammed**, your skill tree should focus on mana regeneration and mobility. Since the Mana Sword scales with Intellect, your stat points should be heavily invested there, with remaining points in Dexterity for speed.

### Recommended Skill Points
1.  **Wizardry:** Essential for mana management since your dash and sword both consume mana.
2.  **Ineffable:** Provides a significant boost to magical properties.
3.  **Rally Ace:** Necessary for healing, as this build has very low base HP due to the Cursed Null accessory.
4.  **Athlete:** Increases movement speed, allowing you to outrun almost any boss in the game.

> **💡 Tip:** Use the "Diver Armor" passive wisely. Once you spend 70 mana, your costs are halved. Time your lightning dashes during this window to cover massive distances for almost no cost.

## Advanced Combat Tactics

Operating a **spark scale pilgrammed** build requires a "hit and run" mentality. Because the Cursed Null makes you a "glass tank" (high damage, very low health), you cannot afford to take direct hits from end-game bosses.

*   **Mana Cycling:** Start the fight by using your Mana Sword to trigger the Diver Armor's cost reduction. Once active, spam the Shock Runner dash to stay behind the enemy.
*   **Parrying:** Even with high speed, you must master parrying. Use the Rally Ace skill to regain health quickly if you do take a hit.
*   **Boss Melting:** Against stationary bosses like the Arch Mage, the combination of Spark Scale and Mana Sword can "melt" their health bars in seconds. Stay close, spam your light attacks, and dash away the moment the boss begins a telegraph.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly does the Big Fish spawn for the Spark Scale?**

A: The Big Fish spawns in the deep ocean. You can often find it by sailing far from the main islands or by being teleported via a whirlpool in the sea. It is a random encounter, so you may need to sail for a few minutes to trigger the spawn.

**Q: Can I use the Spark Scale with a Strength build?**

A: While you can, it is not recommended. The **spark scale pilgrammed** specifically buffs lightning damage, which scales much better with Intellect and magic-based weapons like the Mana Sword or Shock Blade.

**Q: Is the Cursed Null necessary for the lightning build?**

A: It is optional but highly recommended for experienced players. It increases your magic damage by 25%, which is a massive boost for the Spark Scale's effects. If you find yourself dying too often, replace it with "Unharmed Energy" for more survivability.

**Q: How do I get more Arch Energy for the Mana Sword?**

A: Arch Energy is a guaranteed drop from the Arch Mage boss. Most players will need to defeat him twice to collect the 10 energy required for the sword. Ensure you have decent magic defense before entering his arena.
