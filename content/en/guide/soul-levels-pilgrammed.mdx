## Essential Movement Upgrades for 2026

Mobility is the meta in 2026. Without the proper movement skills, you will find yourself unable to reach the white orbs or the higher platforms of the Mountain region. There are three primary movement masters you must visit to round out your character's kit.

### 1. The Climbing Claws (Climb Master)
To obtain the Climbing Claws movement upgrade, head to the Mountain region. Locate the cabin and move to the right, heading toward the Duke of Rocks. 
*   **Location:** Mountain (Near the Ice King's arena)
*   **NPC:** Duke of Rocks
*   **Effect:** Allows you to scale vertical surfaces with significantly less stamina drain.

### 2. Dash Master (Garth)
The Dash Master skill is essential for dodging high-damage boss attacks. You can find Garth at the Elite Brax location. 
*   **Requirement:** While requirements can vary based on your current soul level, most players can unlock this by simply speaking to Garth after reaching the mid-game.
*   **Effect:** Provides a quick directional burst, which can be performed up to three times in quick succession when combined with other upgrades.

### 3. Jump Master (Cloud Boots/Scurry Boots)
The final piece of the movement puzzle is found in Cloud City. You must speak with Adagio (or Adagio) located near the slingshot area. This upgrade is often referred to as the "Scurry Boots" tech.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The journey to Cloud City is dangerous. It is highly recommended to have a **Sport Shawl** equipped to deal with annoying mob aggro and a **Noxious Motor** for vertical propulsion.

## The Great Red Bird Feather Quest

To complete the Cloud City movement upgrade, you need specific materials that are difficult to obtain for those unprepared for high-altitude combat. You will need to gather 20 World Rubber and the rare Great Red Bird Feather.

### Step-by-Step Feather Acquisition:
1.  **Prepare your Glider:** You cannot use the white orbs without having your glider held out.
2.  **Locate the White Orbs:** These are situated on the outskirts of Cloud City.
3.  **Use the Noxious Motor:** If you find the natural propellers aren't giving you enough height, the Noxious Motor is the most reliable way to reach the upper islands.
4.  **Avoid the Tendrilings:** These mobs are the primary cause of death in this zone. Use Sandy Boots for quick movement if you get grounded.
5.  **Pluck the Feather:** Once you reach the highest island, you will find a massive Red Bird. Interact with it to pluck the feather.

| Material | Quantity | Location |
|---|---|---|
| **World Rubber** | 20 | Cloud City (Common Drop/Gather) |
| **Cloud Boots** | 1 | Standard Crafting/Drop |
| **Great Red Bird Feather**| 1 | Top Island (Requires Glider + Orbs) |

## Advanced Movement Techniques (Prejunks)

Once you have unlocked Climb Master, Dash Master, and Jump Master, you can perform "Prejunks." These are advanced movement combos that allow you to bypass entire platforming sections.

1.  **The Triple Dash:** By timing your dashes with the Climbing Claws, you can reset your momentum mid-air.
2.  **Orb Launching:** Holding the glider while hitting a white orb at the apex of a Noxious Motor jump will propel you further than any standard mount.
3.  **Scurry Tech:** Using the Jump Master upgrade allows for a "scurry" effect, which increases horizontal movement speed significantly after landing a jump.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I check which movement upgrades I have unlocked?**

A: You can check your progress in your character menu. Look for the tags "Climb Master," "Dash Master," and "Jump Master" to confirm you have successfully talked to the respective NPCs and turned in the required items.

**Q: Is the Ice King the only way to increase my soul levels pilgrammed?**

A: No, but the Ice King's reworked Soul Fragment is a major component of the 2026 progression path. You should also seek out other world bosses, though the Ice King is currently the most efficient for mid-to-late game players.

**Q: What happens if I fall off Cloud City while trying to get the Feather?**

A: If you fall, you will likely take fatal damage unless you have a glider equipped. Always keep your glider in your hotbar and ensure your Noxious Motor is fueled. If you find the Tendrilings too difficult, consider using the Sport Shawl to reduce their aggro range.

**Q: Do I need the Climbing Claws item to get the movement upgrade?**

A: No. While there is a Climbing Claws item, the movement upgrade given by the Duke of Rocks is a permanent skill that does not take up an accessory slot, making it much more valuable for your **soul levels pilgrammed** journey.
