## Step-by-Step: How to Get Great Red Feather Pilgrammed

The process of **how to get great red feather pilgrammed** involves a specific route through the Imperial Knight territory. Follow these steps to reach the destination:

1.  **Locate the Imperial Knights:** Head to the island inhabited by the Imperial Knights. This is a high-level area, so ensure your armor is up to par.

4.  **Activate the White Orbs:** Look for glowing white orbs in the sky. When you equip your glider and pass through these orbs, they launch you upward and forward.

## Surviving the Tangerine Ambush

As you reach the final set of white orbs, the game will spawn Tangerine enemies. These enemies are designed to knock you out of the air, causing you to fail the sequence. 

Understanding the combat mechanics here is vital for **how to get great red feather pilgrammed**. Note that you cannot parry while gliding or using the orbs effectively. Instead, you must focus on dodging. If a Tangerine hits you, the hit-stun will cancel your glider, leading to a long fall. 

> **💡 Tip:** Keep your camera panned backward occasionally to see if a Tangerine is lunging. A quick mid-air shift can save your run.

## Claiming the Feather and the Skill

Once you reach the very top of the floating structures, you will find a large, sleeping Redbird. You do not need to fight this creature. Simply interact with it to "steal" the Great Redbird Feather. 

After you have the feather, 20 Royal Rubber, and your Cloud Boots, return to the NPC with the slingshot. This NPC is responsible for teaching you the final movement skill.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get the Great Redbird Feather without the Cloud Boots?**

A: You can physically reach the bird and collect the feather without the boots, but the NPC will not grant you the skill until you have the Cloud Boots in your inventory. It is best to buy them beforehand to save a trip.

**Q: What happens if I die after picking up the feather?**

A: The Great Redbird Feather is a key item. Once it is in your inventory, you generally keep it even if you fall or die, though you should check your inventory immediately to ensure it saved.

**Q: Where exactly is the Slingshot NPC located?**

A: The Slingshot NPC is located near the initial shop vendor where you buy the Cloud Boots. He is the one who requests all the materials (Feather, Rubber, and Boots) to teach you the final movement technique.

**Q: Is there any other way to get the Great Red Feather in Pilgrammed?**

A: Currently, stealing it from the sleeping Redbird at the end of the orb sequence is the only legitimate way to obtain the item. It cannot be traded between players or purchased from a standard vendor.
