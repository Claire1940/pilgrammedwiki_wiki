## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to get a soul fragment pilgrammed?**

A: Currently, the fastest method is farming the King Sandworm in the Desert. Because the boss has a low health pool and a predictable move set, high-level players can defeat it in under 30 seconds, leading to a high volume of fragments over an hour of play.

**Q: Can I trade Soul Fragments with other players?**

A: No, Soul Fragments are account-bound materials. You cannot trade them directly with other players. However, you can help friends defeat bosses so they can earn their own drops.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many Soul Fragments I can hold?**

A: As of the latest 2026 updates, there is no known hard cap on the number of fragments you can carry. Many veteran players keep hundreds in reserve for when new "Soul" tier equipment is released in seasonal updates.

**Q: Does the "Luck" stat affect soul fragment pilgrammed drop rates?**

A: Yes, having a higher Luck stat or using items like the Clover Amulet slightly increases the chance of receiving the maximum amount of fragments a boss can drop. While it won't make a boss drop 50 fragments, it ensures you more frequently hit the 3-5 range instead of the 1-2 range.
