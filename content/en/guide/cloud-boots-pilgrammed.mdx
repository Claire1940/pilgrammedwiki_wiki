## Understanding the Cloud Boots Upgrade

In the current version of Pilgrammed, movement upgrades are categorized into specific "Mastery" skills: Climb Master, Dash Master, and Jump Master. The Cloud Boots represent a significant leap in the Jump Master category. Unlike standard equipment that takes up an armor slot, these upgrades function as permanent enhancements to your character's base movement capabilities.

The Cloud Boots are particularly prized for their synergy with other movement items like the Noxious Motor or the Sport Shawl. When combined, these items allow players to skip difficult platforming sections or stay airborne long enough to avoid ground-based area-of-effect (AoE) attacks from bosses like the Ice King or the Elite Brax.

| **Cloud Boots** | Jump Master | Enhanced Jump Height/Air Tech | Cloud City (Adagio) |
| **Climbing Claws** | Climb Master | Vertical Wall Scaling | Mountain (Duke of Rocks) |
| **Dash Master** | Dash Master | Triple Dash Capability | Elite Brax Area (Garth) |

## How to Obtain Cloud Boots Pilgrammed

To begin your journey toward the **cloud boots pilgrammed** upgrade, you must first reach Cloud City. This area is notoriously difficult to navigate due to the high altitude and the presence of "Tendrilings"—hostile entities that can easily knock an unprepared player off the narrow platforms.

### Step 1: Locating Adagio
The NPC responsible for granting the Cloud Boots tech is named **Adagio** (sometimes referred to by players as Adagio). You can find them stationed near the central slingshot hub in Cloud City. Because Cloud City is essentially a series of floating islands connected by slingshots, you will need to familiarize yourself with the launch patterns to find Adagio's specific platform.

### Step 2: Gathering the Materials
Adagio does not provide the upgrade for free. You will need to present two specific items to complete the trade. These requirements have remained consistent through the 2026 updates:

1.  **20 World Rubber**: This is a common material found throughout the mid-to-late game islands. Most players will naturally accumulate this through standard exploration and breaking crates.
2.  **1 Great Red Bird Feather**: This is the "prestige" ingredient that requires a specific mini-quest to obtain.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt to reach the Great Red Bird without a Glider and a way to boost your verticality, such as the Noxious Motor. The platforming required is unforgiving.

## Hunting the Great Red Bird Feather

The Great Red Bird is not a traditional boss you fight with weapons, but rather a navigational challenge. You must travel to a remote island located even higher than the main Cloud City hub.

### Navigating the White Orbs
To reach the bird's roost, you must utilize the **White Orbs** scattered in the sky. These orbs function as jump pads, but there is a specific mechanic involved: you must have your **Glider** equipped and active while passing through them to receive the vertical boost. 

Follow this sequence to reach the top:
1.  Locate the first white orb near the edge of the Cloud City outskirts.
2.  Deploy your Glider and hit the orb to be propelled upward.
3.  Look for the next orb in the sequence. There are three primary orbs you must hit to reach the final peak.
4.  Avoid the Tendrilings during your flight; getting hit mid-air will cancel your glide and likely result in a fatal fall.

Once you reach the highest point, you will see the Great Red Bird. You do not need to kill it—simply interact with it to "pluck" the feather. Once you have the feather, return to Adagio to finalize your **cloud boots pilgrammed** upgrade.

Because Cloud City is filled with enemies that can one-shot lower-level players or knock them into the void, your build is crucial. Many veteran players recommend a high-mobility or long-range build to handle the threats while platforming.

## Synergy with Other Movement Upgrades

The **cloud boots pilgrammed** upgrade is just one piece of the mobility puzzle. To truly master the game's traversal, you should pair it with the other two "Mastery" upgrades introduced in the Prejunks update.

### Climbing Claws (Climb Master)
Located in the Mountain region, near the cabin where the Ice King resides. Talk to the **Duke of Rocks** to receive this skill. It allows you to cling to vertical surfaces, which is a perfect safety net if your Cloud Boots jump doesn't quite reach the top of a platform.

### Dash Master
Obtained by speaking to **Garth** in the Elite Brax area. This allows for three consecutive dashes. When combined with the jump height of the Cloud Boots, you can cover massive horizontal distances in the air, effectively allowing you to fly across entire map sections.

> **💡 Tip:** You can check your current mastery levels in the "Tech" or "Skills" menu. Look for Climb Master, Dash Master, and Jump Master to confirm your upgrades are active.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find World Rubber for the cloud boots pilgrammed upgrade?**

A: World Rubber is found in crates and as drops from mechanical or urban-themed enemies throughout the world. Most players find they have enough (20) by the time they reach Cloud City naturally.

**Q: Does the Great Red Bird kill you if you try to take the feather?**

A: The bird itself is passive, but the journey to reach it is filled with Tendrilings that can one-shot you. The challenge is the platforming, not the bird itself.

**Q: Can I use the Cloud Boots if I don't have the Jump Master skill?**

A: The Cloud Boots *are* the upgrade that grants or enhances the Jump Master status. Once you talk to Adagio and provide the materials, the skill is permanently added to your character.

**Q: What happens if I fall off Cloud City while trying to get the feather?**

A: Unless you have a life-saving item or the Noxious Motor to boost back up, falling usually results in death and a respawn at your last set spawn point. It is highly recommended to set your spawn point at the Cloud City entrance before attempting the feather run.
