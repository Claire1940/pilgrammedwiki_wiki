Executing the rotation for the **vine wand build pilgrammed** requires more skill than a standard "spam-to-win" build. You must manage your distance and timing to ensure the vines stack correctly.

## Solo vs. Group Play Strategies

The way you play the **vine wand build pilgrammed** changes depending on whether you have a team. This build is incredibly versatile, but it shines brightest when you have a dedicated support player.

In solo play, the burden of application is entirely on you. You will need to be very precise with your timing. Most players recommend focusing on the "Mana Harmonizer" variant for solo runs. This allows you to stack vines more frequently, which compensates for the lack of a secondary player's status applications. ### Group Strategy
In a group setting, the Vine Wand user becomes the primary "Damage Over Time" (DOT) carry. One teammate should focus on applying Pink Slime and keeping the boss's aggro, while the Vine Wand user stays at mid-range, constantly refreshing the vine stacks. In this scenario, it is possible to see poison ticks exceeding 50,000 damage per second, effectively deleting even the tankiest endgame bosses.
