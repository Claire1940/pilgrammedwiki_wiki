## Where to Find Toolmark Pilgrammed

The primary challenge in locating Toolmark is navigating the Savannah biome. The Savannah is a large, open area characterized by its golden grass, sparse trees, and dangerous wildlife. To find the NPC, you must set your sights on a specific sub-area known as Tail Fish Island. This island is located off the coast of the main Savannah landmass and requires a bit of a trek to reach.

When you enter the Savannah, you should head toward the coastline. Look out into the water for a landmass that resembles the tail of a fish—hence the name. Toolmark stands out in the open on this island, waiting for travelers to browse his wares.

## Toolmark's Shop Inventory and Accessories

Once you have successfully reached the **toolmark pilgrammed** NPC, you will have access to a unique shop that focuses heavily on mechanical utility and ranged combat improvements. Unlike general merchants, Toolmark specializes in "Expert" gear and reloading mechanics, making him a mandatory stop for any player utilizing firearms or complex tool-based builds.

The items sold here are designed to enhance your efficiency in combat. For example, the "Quick Loader" is a staple for anyone tired of the long downtime between shots with heavy ranged weapons. The "Expert" accessory is equally valuable, providing stat boosts that favor players who have mastered the game's timing and precision mechanics.

| Accessory Name | Primary Benefit | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Expert** | Precision/Stat Boosts | Advanced Combat Builds |

Interacting with the **toolmark pilgrammed** shop requires gold, so make sure you have farmed enough from the Savannah mobs before making the trip. The prices are fair for the utility they provide, but they can be a steep investment for players who haven't yet reached the mid-game farming routes.

## Comparing Toolmark and Fang

In the world of Pilgrammed, Toolmark is often mentioned alongside another secret vendor named Fang. While Toolmark is found in the Savannah, Fang resides in the Swamp. Understanding the difference between these two is vital for build optimization. While the **toolmark pilgrammed** inventory focuses on mechanical and ranged utility, Fang is the go-to source for dagger accessories and blade-focused items like the Elf Blade.

If you are running a melee-centric build, particularly one focusing on speed and critical hits with daggers, you may find Fang's inventory more appealing. However, for those who prefer a tactical, distance-based approach, Toolmark remains the superior choice.

> **Warning:** Both locations (Swamp and Savannah) contain environmental hazards. The Swamp has poison and obscured vision, while the Savannah has wide-open spaces that make you an easy target for roaming predators.

## How to Prepare for the Savannah Trek

Reaching the **toolmark pilgrammed** NPC isn't just about knowing the coordinates; it's about surviving the journey. The Savannah is home to some of the more frustrating mobs in the early-to-mid game. To ensure a successful trip to Tail Fish Island, consider the following preparations:

1.  **Mobility Skills:** Having a dash or a double jump can help you navigate the terrain and avoid the aggro range of lions and other Savannah beasts.
2.  **Health Pots:** Even if you are high level, the chip damage from environmental hazards or unexpected ambushes can add up.
3.  **Gold Reserve:** There is nothing more frustrating than reaching a secret vendor only to realize you are short on currency. Bring at least a few thousand gold to cover multiple purchases.

The path to Toolmark is a straight shot once you recognize the landmarks. By heading directly forward from the main Savannah entrance toward the sea, Tail Fish Island will eventually render in the distance. The island itself is relatively safe compared to the mainland, allowing you to browse the shop in peace.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is Toolmark located in Pilgrammed?**

A: You can find **toolmark pilgrammed** in the Savannah biome. He is standing on Tail Fish Island, which is located off the coast. You can see the island from the mainland if you look toward the horizon from the Savannah's edge.

**Q: What items does Toolmark sell?**

A: Toolmark sells specialized accessories, most notably the "Expert" and the "Quick Loader." These items are designed to help with reload speeds and general combat efficiency for advanced players.

**Q: Is Toolmark better than Fang?**

A: Neither is objectively "better" as they serve different builds. Toolmark is best for ranged and mechanical builds, while Fang (located in the Swamp) is better for players using daggers and fast melee weapons like the Elf Blade.

**Q: Do I need a boat to reach Tail Fish Island?**

A: While you can swim to the island, having a mobility skill or a high movement speed makes the trip much faster and safer from any aquatic threats that might be lurking in the Savannah waters.
