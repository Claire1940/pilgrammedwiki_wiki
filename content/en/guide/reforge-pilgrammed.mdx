## The Mechanics of Reforging

Reforging is a straightforward yet deep mechanic. When you approach a designated Smith or Reforge NPC, you can place a piece of equipment into the reforge slot. By spending a specific amount of gold, the item's current modifier is replaced with a new, randomized one. This process can be repeated indefinitely, provided you have the currency to support it.

The cost of each reforge typically scales with the rarity and base power of the item. A starter sword might only cost a few dozen gold to reforge, while endgame artifacts can require thousands of gold per attempt. This makes gold farming an essential secondary activity for anyone serious about their gear progression.

### Common Reforge Modifiers and Their Effects

Not all modifiers are created equal. Some provide massive boosts to damage, while others focus on utility like attack speed or critical hit chance. Conversely, some modifiers can actually weaken your gear, such as the "Broken" or "Shoddy" prefixes.

| Modifier | Primary Stat Boost | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Legendary** | Massive Damage, Speed, and Crit | All-around DPS |
| **Godly** | High Damage and Crit Chance | Burst Damage Builds |
| **Wonderful** | Balanced Stat Increase | General Progression |
| **Heavy** | High Damage, Reduced Speed | Strength-based Builds |
| **Light** | Increased Speed, Reduced Damage | Fast-hitting Weapons |
| **Broken** | Significant Stat Penalties | Avoid at all costs |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Always keep a reserve of gold before you start a reforge session. There is a risk of landing on a "Broken" modifier and running out of money, leaving your main weapon weaker than when you started.

## Strategies for Getting Legendary Gear

The "Legendary" modifier is widely considered the gold standard for most builds. It provides a comprehensive suite of buffs that improve almost every facet of a weapon's performance. However, because the system is RNG-based (Random Number Generation), hitting a Legendary roll can be difficult.

To optimize your chances, follow these steps:

1.  **Accumulate Wealth:** Do not start rolling for Legendary until you have at least 10-20 times the cost of a single reforge.
2.  **Identify "Wonderful" as a Safety Net:** As noted by experienced players, the "Wonderful" modifier is an excellent middle-ground. If you hit Wonderful and your gold is running low, stop there. It is better than most other rolls and will serve you well until you can afford more attempts.
3.  **Prioritize Your Main Weapon:** Focus your resources on your primary damage-dealing tool before worrying about secondary items or armor.
4.  **Tool Reforging:** Don't forget that tools (like pickaxes) can also be reforged. A "Light" or "Fast" pickaxe can significantly speed up your resource gathering.

## Advanced Reforging: Building for Your Class

In 2026, the meta for Pilgrammed has shifted toward highly specialized classes. This means that a "Legendary" roll might not always be the absolute best choice for every specific build. For example, a tank-focused build might prefer modifiers that increase knockback or defense over raw attack speed.

### Melee Builds
Melee users should generally aim for **Legendary** or **Godly**. These provide the raw power needed to clear mobs quickly. If you are using a slow, heavy weapon like a hammer, the **Heavy** modifier can lean into your strengths, making every hit feel devastating.

### Magic and Ranged Builds
For those utilizing magic staves or bows, modifiers that affect projectile speed and mana consumption (if applicable) are paramount. While the reforge system for magic items functions the same way, the stat priorities differ slightly. Look for modifiers that reduce the "wind-up" time of your spells.

> **💡 Tip:** If you are playing a "Glass Cannon" build (high damage, low health), prioritize the **Godly** modifier for the maximum possible critical hit chance.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I lose my item if I reforge it too many times?**

A: No, the item itself will never be destroyed during the reforge process. Only the modifier (the prefix) changes. Your base item is safe, though its stats may temporarily decrease if you roll a negative modifier.

**Q: Does the NPC location affect the chance of getting a Legendary reforge in Pilgrammed?**

A: There is no official evidence suggesting that specific NPCs have higher "luck" than others. Most players choose their reforge location based on convenience and proximity to their current questing area.

**Q: Is "Godly" better than "Legendary"?**

A: This depends on your build. "Legendary" is generally better for overall speed and consistent damage, while "Godly" often provides higher critical hit stats. For most players, either one is considered a "perfect" roll.

**Q: How do I get more gold to reforge pilgrammed gear?**

A: The best ways to farm gold in 2026 include completing daily bounties, selling unwanted mob drops to vendors, and clearing high-level dungeons with a party. Consistent farming is the only way to afford late-game reforging.
