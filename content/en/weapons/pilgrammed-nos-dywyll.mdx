## How to Obtain the Nos Dywyll in 2026

Acquiring the **pilgrammed nos dywyll** is no simple feat. It is generally considered a mid-to-late game item that requires players to have already conquered several major bosses. 1. **Defeat the Shadow Guardian:** This boss has a low percentage chance to drop the "Hollowed Handle," a core component for the scythe.

4. **Refining the Blade:** Once crafted, it is highly recommended to use a "Malice Stone" to give the weapon its initial shadow infusion.
