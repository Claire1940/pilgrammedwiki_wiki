## Combat Strategies: NPCs vs. Bosses

Using **pilgrammed daggers** effectively requires a shift in mindset depending on your target. You cannot approach a world boss the same way you approach a group of low-level bandits.

### Shredding NPCs and Farming
For standard mob farming, the strategy is simple: **Teleport and Spam.** 
*   Initiate with a Heavy Attack to teleport directly onto the NPC.
*   Spam your M1 attacks. Because the third M1 also teleports, even if the NPC tries to knock you back, you will often "stick" to them.
*   Use the **Relentless** reforge to ensure your swing speed is fast enough to stun-lock weaker enemies.

### Boss Soloing Techniques
Bosses like the Thief King or the Shock Marauder require more finesse. You cannot simply spam M1s, or you will be punished during their hyper-armor frames.
*   **Wait for Vulnerability:** Every boss has a "cool down" period after a big attack. This is your window.
*   **The Teleport Strike:** Use your heavy attack teleport to enter the fray the moment the boss finishes an animation.
*   **Parry is King:** Dagger builds have low health compared to hammer or greatsword builds. You must master the parry timing. If you have **Ace Rally** equipped, a single successful parry can reset the momentum of the fight in your favor.

## FAQ

**Q: Why should I use the Pro Dagger over other pilgrammed daggers?**

A: The Pro Dagger is unique because of its dual-teleport mechanic. Both the heavy attack and the third M1 hit allow you to instantly close the gap with enemies. This eliminates the "dead time" spent walking toward targets, significantly increasing your effective DPS.

**Q: Is this build viable for players who are bad at parrying?**

A: While parrying is a core mechanic of *Pilgrammed*, this build is more forgiving than most. By using the **Bloodthirst** skill and the **Tank/Tenacity** defensive nodes, you can survive more hits. However, for endgame bosses, you will eventually need to learn parry timings to succeed.

**Q: Can I use the Diamond Ring instead of the Shark Tooth Necklace?**

A: Yes. While the Shark Tooth Necklace is often considered "Best in Slot" for raw damage, the Diamond Ring provides excellent all-around stats that make it a very strong secondary choice, especially if you haven't had luck with rare drops yet.

**Q: What is the best gem for a beginner dagger build?**

A: If you don't have the **Insignia** gem yet, a simple Ruby or Emerald gem can provide basic damage or speed boosts. However, you should aim to acquire the Insignia as soon as possible to take full advantage of the Pro Dagger's high swing speed.
