In the unforgiving world of Pilgrammed, your equipment is the only thing standing between you and a swift trip back to the starting village. As you progress through 2026, simply finding a high-damage weapon isn't enough; you must optimize it using the **pilgrammed reforge** system. This mechanic allows players to spend currency to reroll the innate modifiers on their weapons and tools, potentially turning a mediocre blade into a Godly or Legendary powerhouse.

Understanding how to effectively use the **pilgrammed reforge** system is the difference between struggling with early-game mobs and breezing through late-game challenges. Whether you are looking for the "Wonderful" modifier to balance your build or hunting for that elusive "Legendary" tag to maximize your DPS, this guide covers everything from NPC locations to cost-effective strategies for high-tier gear.

## Master the Pilgrammed Reforge Mechanics

Reforging in Pilgrammed is a straightforward but high-stakes process. Every major settlement and town features a specific NPC dedicated to this service. These blacksmith-like characters allow you to place an item in a slot and pay a fee to change its prefix. This prefix provides significant stat boosts or, in some unfortunate cases, detrimental debuffs.

The goal for most players is to reach the higher tiers of modifiers. According to veteran players, the **Wonderful** modifier is widely considered the best all-around choice for general builds because it provides a balanced boost to several key stats without the extreme rarity of other top-tier rolls. However, if you have the gold and the patience, the **Legendary** modifier remains the gold standard for pure power.

| Reforge Tier | General Effectiveness | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Legendary** | Extreme | Maximum Damage and Crit |
| **Wonderful** | High | All-around balanced builds |
| **Godly** | High | High burst damage |
| **Heavy** | Moderate | Slow, high-impact weapons |
| **Sharp** | Moderate | Fast, low-damage weapons |

> **💡 Tip:** Don't waste all your gold reforging early-game weapons. Save your resources for mid-to-late game gear that you plan on keeping for a significant amount of time.

## Finding the Reforge NPCs

One of the most convenient aspects of the game is that a **pilgrammed reforge** NPC is located in almost every town. This ensures that you never have to travel too far to upgrade your kit after a successful raid or boss fight. In the starting village, you can find the basic reforge NPC near the main square, while more specialized NPCs appear in later areas.

It is important to note that some NPCs specialize in different types of equipment. While most can handle standard weapons, certain characters are better suited for reforging tools. If you are looking to improve your mining speed or woodcutting efficiency, seek out the tool-specific reforger to ensure you aren't wasting currency on the wrong table.

| Town/Location | NPC Type | Primary Service |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Starting Village** | General Reforger | Basic weapons and tools |
| **Desert Outpost** | Combat Specialist | High-tier weapon modifiers |
| **Volcano Region** | Tool Reforger | Mining and gathering tools |
| **Cloud City** | Advanced Reforger | Legendary and Godly rolls |

## Obtaining High-Value Gear for Reforging

Before you spend your hard-earned gold on a **pilgrammed reforge**, you need a weapon worth the investment. One of the best early-to-mid game options is the heavy weapon dropped by the boss known as Granny. This boss is located in the Volcano area, a region that can be reached by following a specific path through the desert.

To find Granny, head straight from the starting village into the desert. Look for a "seizure" or a small crack in the rock wall that leads into the Volcano biome. Once inside, you will find a large chasm. Jumping down will trigger the boss fight. Be prepared to take some fall damage upon entry, so ensure your health is topped off.

### Granny Boss Fight Strategy

Granny is a formidable opponent for the unprepared, but she follows predictable patterns. Success in this fight relies heavily on your ability to use the **F key** to parry her attacks.

1.  **Parry the Kick:** When Granny winds up for a kick, time your parry to stagger her.
2.  **Dodge the Slam:** Her overhead slam has an awkward parry window; it is often safer to dodge-roll out of the way.
3.  **Vulnerability Window:** When Granny performs her rolling attack, she is extremely vulnerable once she stops. This is your best opening to deal massive damage.
4.  **The Reward:** Defeating her grants a heavy weapon with a base damage of 40. This is an excellent candidate for a **pilgrammed reforge** to carry you into the late game.

| Weapon Stat | Base Value | With Legendary Reforge |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Damage** | 40 | ~52-56 |
| **Attack Speed** | Slow | Moderate |
| **Crit Chance** | 5% | ~15% |
| **Weight** | Heavy | Heavy |

## Advanced Reforging Strategies

As you approach the endgame in 2026, the cost of each **pilgrammed reforge** will increase based on the rarity and power of the item. To avoid going broke, follow these professional tips:

*   **The "Good Enough" Rule:** If you roll a "Wonderful" modifier, it is often better to stop there rather than chasing a "Legendary" roll and ending up with a "Broken" or "Weak" modifier when your gold runs out.
*   **Tool Efficiency:** Don't forget to reforge your tools. A "Fast" or "Light" modifier on a pickaxe can significantly reduce the time spent farming ores.
*   **Synergy Matters:** If you are running a heavy armor build, look for modifiers that increase your poise or flat damage. If you are a glass cannon, focus entirely on crit chance and speed.

For more information on the latest updates and community events, check out the [official Pilgrammed page on Roblox](https://www.roblox.com/games/11130155629/Pilgrammed-BETA), where you can find links to the Discord server and patch notes.

> **Warning:** Reforging is a random process. There is no guarantee that spending more gold will result in a better modifier. Always keep enough gold in reserve for basic necessities like potions and travel.

## Step-by-Step Reforging Process

If you are new to the game, follow these steps to upgrade your equipment:

1.  **Accumulate Gold:** Ensure you have at least 500-1,000 gold before attempting multiple reforges on mid-tier gear.
2.  **Locate the NPC:** Find the blacksmith icon on your map in any major town.
3.  **Select Your Item:** Interact with the NPC and drag your weapon into the designated reforge slot.
4.  **Confirm the Cost:** Check the price per roll. This price stays static for that specific item but varies between different weapon tiers.
5.  **Roll and Observe:** Click the reforge button. Watch the prefix change. If you hit "Legendary" or "Wonderful," it is highly recommended to stop.

| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Farm Gold | High |
| **2** | Visit Town NPC | Mandatory |
| **3** | Slot Item | Mandatory |
| **4** | Check Modifier | High |
| **5** | Save/Retry | Strategic |

## FAQ

**Q: Can I lose my weapon during a pilgrammed reforge?**

A: No, you cannot lose the item itself. However, you can "ruin" the stats temporarily by rolling a negative modifier like "Broken," "Dull," or "Weak." You will need to reforge again to remove these negative effects.

**Q: What is the best modifier for heavy weapons?**

A: While "Legendary" is the most powerful, many players prefer "Wonderful" for heavy weapons because it provides a boost to attack speed, which helps mitigate the natural slowness of heavy gear.

**Q: Does the reforge cost increase every time I click the button?**

A: No, the cost to reforge a specific item remains constant regardless of how many times you reroll it. However, higher-quality base weapons (like those dropped by late-game bosses) cost significantly more per roll than starter swords.

**Q: Where is the best place to farm gold for reforging?**

A: Farming the Granny boss in the Volcano area is a popular method. Not only does she drop a valuable weapon, but the surrounding mobs and the boss herself provide a steady stream of currency for your **pilgrammed reforge** needs.
