## Stat Priorities for a Pilgrammed Healer Build

To maximize the effectiveness of your **pilgrammed healer build**, you must distribute your stat points with precision. While Intelligence is the primary driver for magic-based healing, other stats like Charisma and Vitality play crucial roles in your overall performance.

| Stat | Priority | Benefit for Healers |
|---|---|---|
| **Intelligence (INT)** | High | Increases the potency of magic-based heals and mana pool. |
| **Charisma (CHR)** | High | Scales specific support items and party-wide buff durations. |
| **Vitality (VIT)** | Medium | Provides the health cushion needed to survive stray hits. |
| **Dexterity (DEX)** | Low | Useful for cast speed and specific weapon requirements. |

When leveling up, aim for a balanced approach between INT and CHR. Intelligence ensures that your "burst" heals can top off a tank's health instantly, while Charisma ensures your passive auras and buffs remain active for the duration of long encounters.

## Advanced Tactics and Team Synergy

To truly excel with a **pilgrammed healer build**, you must coordinate with your team. Communication is key. You should always let your frontline know when your major cooldowns are available.

1. **The "Healing Circle" Strategy**: Place your Holy Staff zones in areas where the boss is likely to stay stationary. Encourage your melee fighters to stack within the circle to maximize the efficiency of your mana.
2. **Mana Management**: Don't spam your most expensive heals on minor scratches. Use your lower-cost abilities for chip damage and save your burst heals for "red-line" situations.
3. **Buff Rotation**: If you are using Charisma-based buffs, try to stagger them. Instead of using all your damage boosts at once, rotate them to ensure the team has a consistent power level throughout the fight.

## FAQ

**Q: Is a pilgrammed healer build viable for solo play?**

A: While it is possible to solo some content with a healer build, it is significantly slower than a dedicated DPS build. Most healers use a "hybrid" approach for soloing, swapping some support accessories for damage-focused ones.

**Q: Which stat is more important: Intelligence or Charisma?**

A: Intelligence is generally more important for raw healing numbers, while Charisma is better for party buffs and specific item scaling. For a balanced **pilgrammed healer build**, you should aim to have a healthy mix of both.

**Q: Can I heal myself with the Holy Staff?**

A: Yes, most area-of-effect healing abilities in the game will affect the caster as well as allies. This makes the Holy Staff an excellent tool for self-sustain during difficult boss fights.

**Q: What is the best secondary weapon for a healer?**

A: A fast melee weapon or a shield is recommended. A shield can help you survive heavy hits if you miss a dodge, while a fast weapon allows you to pick off low-health enemies without consuming mana.
