## Locating the Granny Boss in the Desert

Before you can face the **pilgrammed granny**, you must first navigate the harsh desert environment. The desert is home to various threats, but your primary goal is to reach the town located within this sandy expanse. For those just starting their adventure, it is often best to avoid unnecessary combat with roaming enemies to conserve health and resources.

Once you arrive at the desert town, your first priority should be locating the Mirror. The Mirror serves as a vital checkpoint; given the difficulty of the boss and the risks involved in the encounter, having a nearby respawn point is critical. From the town, you will need to locate a large pit. This pit is the entrance to Granny's lair.

| Location Feature | Description | Importance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Desert Town** | The central hub of the biome. | Contains the Mirror checkpoint. |
| **The Mirror** | A respawn point located in town. | Saves time if you fail the boss fight. |
| **The Pit** | A large hole in the ground near town. | Leads directly to the boss arena. |

> **Warning:** Jumping into the pit will result in significant fall damage. Ensure you have adequate armor equipped and are at full health before making the descent.

## The "Wall Cheese" Strategy

For many players, the most reliable way to secure a victory is by using the "cheese" method. This strategy exploits the boss's movement patterns and the geometry of the arena. The **pilgrammed granny** has a tendency to roll toward the player, which can be used to your advantage.

### Step-by-Step Cheese Instructions

Follow these steps to trap the boss and whittle down her health safely:

| Step | Action | Objective |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Descend into the pit. | Enter the boss arena and trigger the fight. |
| **2** | Position near a wall. | Bait Granny into performing her rolling attack. |
| **3** | Maneuver around the corner. | Force Granny to collide with the small interior walls. |
| **4** | Maintain distance. | Ensure she stays caught on the geometry while you attack. |

Once she is stuck, you can use the Magic Branch or another ranged/long-reach weapon to deal damage. It is important to note that Granny's hitbox can be somewhat inconsistent; some of your attacks may appear to miss even if they look like they should connect. Persistence is key, as this method can take upwards of 30 minutes depending on your damage output.

> **💡 Tip:** If you happen to die during the fight, do not panic. In the current version of the game, Granny's health does not regenerate upon your death, allowing you to return and finish the job.

## Advanced Combat Tactics

If you prefer not to use the cheese method, fighting the **pilgrammed granny** fairly requires a deep understanding of parrying and positioning. Her rolling attack is her most dangerous move, but it also leaves her vulnerable if you can dodge or parry it successfully.

*   **Parry Timing:** Wait for the animation of her roll to reach its peak before attempting a parry.
*   **Stamina Management:** Don't over-commit to attacks. Save enough stamina to dash away if she breaks out of a stagger.
*   **Arena Awareness:** Stay away from the center of the pit where you have less room to maneuver. Use the outer edges to circle-strafe her.

## Progression After the Boss

Defeating Granny opens up several paths for the player. With her hammer in hand, you will find that many of the enemies in the desert and the following forest areas become much easier to manage. The high stagger value of the hammer allows you to interrupt enemy attacks, providing a significant defensive advantage.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the pilgrammed granny located?**

A: She is located at the bottom of a large pit in the Desert biome, near the Town of Mirages. You must jump down into the pit to start the encounter.

**Q: What happens if I die during the fight?**

A: If you have used the Mirror in the desert town, you will respawn nearby. Fortunately, the boss's health does not reset, so you can jump back down and continue the fight from where you left off.

**Q: Is the Magic Branch necessary to win?**

A: It is not strictly necessary, but it is highly recommended for beginners due to its ease of acquisition and effective reach, especially when using the wall cheese strategy.

**Q: How long does the cheese method take?**

A: Depending on your weapon's damage, it can take anywhere from 10 to 30 minutes. The method is slow but very safe for low-level players.
