In the vast and often cryptic world of Roblox's hit RPG, finding every secret requires patience, skill, and a bit of inside knowledge. One of the most elusive encounters players search for is the **pilgrammed old fart**, a hidden boss tucked away in a location most travelers walk right over. This boss isn't just a trophy for completionists; he holds some of the most powerful late-game items for Mages and ranged builds alike.

To successfully challenge the **pilgrammed old fart**, you must navigate a series of parkour challenges, gather specific mining tools, and solve a map-wide puzzle involving hidden mushroom engravings. Whether you are looking for the devastating Spore Shot or the stat-heavy Mushroom Cap, this guide provides the optimal strategy to locate, unlock, and defeat this eccentric foe in 2026. Prepare your gear and head toward the border of the forest, as the path to the Condemned Passage is not for the faint of heart.

## Locating the Condemned Passage

The journey to find the **pilgrammed old fart** begins in an unassuming hole in the ground located between the Forest and the Desert biomes. This area, known as the Condemned Passage, is notoriously difficult to navigate due to heavy fog and darkness.

Before entering, it is highly recommended to visit a bank and deposit your gold. The parkour sections inside are unforgiving, and falling into the abyss is a common occurrence for unprepared players. If you have access to a "Speed Demon" mobility build, now is the time to equip it to make the jumps more manageable.

### Navigating the Interior
Once you drop into the hole, follow these steps to reach the boss door:
1. Enter the first room and take an immediate right.
2. Look for a wooden board propped against the wall; walk through the gap behind it.
3. Complete the foggy parkour section. Take your time, as the low visibility is the primary hazard here.
4. Locate the **Mirror** at the bottom of the parkour area. Activating this is crucial so you don't have to repeat the jumps if you die during the boss fight.
5. Jump down the final opening in the floor to reach the boss's lobby.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The fog in the Condemned Passage has been reported as thicker in recent 2026 updates. Ensure your brightness settings are adjusted or use a light-emitting item if you struggle with the platforming.

## Prerequisites: The Gemstone Pickaxe

You will notice a massive stone door in the lobby engraved with mushroom symbols. This door remains sealed until you find three specific engravings hidden across the world. However, these engravings cannot be activated with standard tools; you must craft the **Gemstone Pickaxe**.

To obtain this tool, head to the Savannah blacksmith. You will need a collection of rare gems found in various dangerous locales.

| Gem Type | Mining Location | Recommended Gear |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ruby** | Volcano | Heat-resistant armor |
| **Emerald** | Desert | High mobility for sand dunes |
| **Sapphire** | Mountain | Cold resistance or climbing gear |

Once you have gathered these materials, craft the Gemstone Pickaxe. This tool is a permanent addition to your inventory and is required for several secret bosses beyond just the **pilgrammed old fart**.

## Solving the Mushroom Puzzle

With your Gemstone Pickaxe ready, you must travel to three distinct locations to "mine" the hidden mushroom engravings. Interacting with these will trigger a message on your screen, indicating that the seal on the boss door is weakening.

### Mushroom Location 1: The Abandoned Lighthouse
Teleport to the Forest mirror and head toward the Abandoned Lighthouse. Look for a small ledge near the base of the structure. On the ground, you will see a tiny mushroom. Pull out your Gemstone Pickaxe and strike the nearby wall to reveal the engraving. Press 'G' to interact with it.

### Mushroom Location 2: The Savannah Island
Teleport to the Savannah and spawn a ship. Sail directly forward until you hit a small, seemingly empty island. Walk around the corner of the rock formation to find the second mushroom. Mine the wall and interact with the engraving.

### Mushroom Location 3: The Cloud City Ledge
Take the slingshot up to Cloud City and pass through the two teleporters. Head behind the large white building and look toward the horizon for a distant island. Use your glider to reach the ledge on the back of this island. The final mushroom engraving is located at the very end of this narrow cliffside.

## Boss Battle: Defeating the Old Fart

After interacting with all three mushrooms, return to the Condemned Passage. The door will now open, leading you to a wide, open field. The **pilgrammed old fart** will not attack immediately, allowing you to prepare your buffs and get the first hit for free.

### Phase 1 Strategy
In the first half of the fight, the boss relies on physical swings and a basic rectangular laser. 
*   **Parrying:** This is your best friend. Almost all of his melee swings are slow and predictable.
*   **Rally Skill:** Equip the Rally skill to regain health quickly upon a successful parry.
*   **Headbutts:** When he leans back for a headbutt, dodge to the side rather than parrying, as the timing can be tricky.

### Phase 2: The Ink Transition
Once his health drops below 50%, he will throw ink on your screen, partially obscuring your vision. He also gains several new laser-based attacks.

| Attack Name | Description | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Laser Pillars** | Beams descend from the sky | Keep moving in a circular pattern |
| **Dual Rectangles** | Two horizontal laser beams | Time your parry or jump over them |
| **Floor Lasers** | The ground glows with energy | Stand close to the boss to bait melee moves |
| **Multi-Directional** | Lasers fire in a star pattern | Zoom out your camera to see the trajectory |

> **💡 Tip:** If you are a melee user, don't get greedy during Phase 2. The ink makes it hard to see his wind-up animations, so focus on defensive parrying until the ink clears.

## Loot and Rewards

Defeating the **pilgrammed old fart** teleports you to a "Strange World." While this area currently serves as a lore-heavy backdrop with little to do, the actual drops from the boss are top-tier for 2026 builds.

| Item | Drop Rate | Type | Key Stats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Spore Shot** | 50% | Gun | 50 Damage, Multi-projectile spread |
| **Shrumerang** | 50% | Melee/Ranged | 30 Damage, Triple-hit Heavy Attack |
| **Mushroom Cap** | 35% | Headwear | **+40 Intellect**, +12 Defense, -30 Agility |

The **Spore Shot** is excellent for proc-based builds, as each small projectile can trigger effects like explosions. The **Mushroom Cap** is arguably the best helmet for late-game Mages, providing a massive boost to Intellect at the cost of some mobility.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the pilgrammed old fart located?**

A: He is located in the Condemned Passage, which can be accessed through a hole in the ground situated between the Forest and Desert biomes. You must complete a parkour section and solve a mushroom puzzle to fight him.

**Q: Why can't I open the mushroom door?**

A: You must find and interact with three hidden mushroom engravings located at the Abandoned Lighthouse, a secret Savannah island, and a ledge behind Cloud City. You need a Gemstone Pickaxe to activate them.

**Q: Is the Mushroom Cap good for melee builds?**

A: Generally, no. While it provides decent defense, the heavy penalties to Agility (-30) and Dexterity (-20) make it poor for melee users. It is designed specifically for Mages who need the +40 Intellect.

**Q: What happens if I fall during the parkour in the Condemned Passage?**

A: If you haven't deposited your money, you will lose a portion of it. However, if you have activated the Mirror at the bottom of the parkour area, you can teleport back there immediately without redo-ing the jumps.
