Navigating the expansive world of Pilgrammed requires more than just raw strength; it demands superior mobility to reach hidden islands and dodge lethal boss mechanics. The "Prejunks" update in 2026 has introduced a suite of movement upgrades that fundamentally change how players interact with the environment. Central to these upgrades is the **pilgrammed great red feather**, a rare quest item essential for unlocking the highly coveted Jump Master skill. This skill grants players a double jump and an extended air dive, making it a "must-have" for both competitive PvP and high-level PvE encounters.

To obtain the **pilgrammed great red feather**, you must venture into the treacherous heights of the sky islands and outmaneuver the aggressive wildlife that guards the Great Red Bird. This guide will walk you through the prerequisites, the optimal traversal routes, and the specific NPC interactions required to turn this feather into a permanent movement buff. Whether you are a veteran explorer or a new pilgrim, mastering the path to the feather is the first step toward true aerial dominance in Pilgrammed.

## What is the Pilgrammed Great Red Feather?

The **pilgrammed great red feather** is a unique crafting and quest component found exclusively in the Cloud City region. Unlike standard drops that you might farm from common mobs, this feather is "plucked" directly from a massive, sleeping avian NPC located on a remote, high-altitude island. 

Its primary purpose is to satisfy the requirements of Adagio (or Adagio), an NPC found near the slingshot in Cloud City. By bringing him this feather along with other materials, players can unlock the "Jump Master" skill. This skill is essentially a permanent version of the Scurry Boots' ability, allowing for a mid-air jump and a dive extension that significantly increases the distance you can cover without touching the ground.

> **💡 Tip:** Do not attempt to sell or discard the feather once you obtain it. It is a one-time quest item that is consumed upon unlocking the skill.

## Prerequisites for the Great Red Feather Quest

Before you set out to find the **pilgrammed great red feather**, you need to ensure your inventory is prepared. Adagio will not grant you the Jump Master skill with the feather alone. You must also possess the necessary footwear and raw materials to complete the "tech" upgrade.

### Required Materials
1.  **Cloud Boots:** These can be purchased from the vendor in Cloud City. They provide the base mobility needed to survive the fall damage and height requirements of the sky islands.
2.  **20 Royal Rubber:** This material is commonly found by defeating Imperial Knights or searching chests in the Royal areas. It is recommended to farm these before heading to the bird's island to save time.
3.  **A Glider:** This is non-negotiable. The path to the feather involves using "White Orbs" that only propel the player if they have their glider equipped and active.

### Recommended Gear
While not strictly required, having the following items will make the journey significantly easier:
*   **Noxious Mortar:** This item is incredibly useful for gaining initial height or bypassing difficult platforming sections.
*   **Sport Shawl:** The "Tendrilings" on the way to the bird are notorious for interrupting player movement. The Sport Shawl helps mitigate some of the annoyance from these enemies.
*   **Sandy Boots:** Useful for quick movement on the ground before you take to the skies.

## Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Great Red Bird

The journey to find the **pilgrammed great red feather** starts in Cloud City. You need to locate the far island where the Great Red Bird rests. There are two primary ways to reach this destination.

### Method 1: The Noxious Mortar Shortcut
If you have the Noxious Mortar, you can launch yourself toward the distant islands from the main Cloud City hub. This bypasses much of the lower-level platforming but requires precise aiming to land on the intermediate islands.

### Method 2: The Imperial Knight Teleporter
For players without the mortar, head to the island inhabited by the Imperial Knights. Past the knights, there is a hidden teleporter located on the lower ledge. Taking this teleporter will bring you to a smaller, isolated island that serves as the starting point for the "White Orb" sequence.

### The White Orb Sequence
Once you are on the starting island, you will see glowing white orbs floating in the air. This is the most technical part of the quest:
1.  **Equip your Glider:** You must be holding the glider for the orbs to work.
2.  **Launch:** Walk into the first orb. It will propel you upward and forward.
3.  **Chain the Orbs:** There are several orbs in a sequence. You must glide from one to the next. Be careful, as the boost from each orb is temporary.
4.  **Avoid Tendrilings:** These flying enemies will attempt to knock you out of the air. Do not try to parry them while gliding; instead, focus on dodging their trajectory to avoid hit-stun.
5.  **Final Ascent:** The last orb will launch you onto a high cliffside. Use your climbing abilities (or Climbing Claws if you have them) to reach the very top of the peak.

> **Warning:** Falling during this sequence often results in a reset to the previous island or a long trek back from the main hub. Ensure your glider is always out!

## Unlocking the Jump Master Skill

Once you reach the summit, you will find the Great Red Bird sleeping. Approach it cautiously—though it won't attack you while it's asleep, the surrounding area is still dangerous. Interact with the bird to "pluck" the **pilgrammed great red feather**. 

With the feather in your inventory, return to Cloud City and speak with Adagio. If you have the Cloud Boots and the 20 Royal Rubber, he will combine them with the feather to grant you the Jump Master skill.

| Skill Name | Input Command | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Double Jump** | Space (in air) | Performs a second jump mid-air for extra height. |
| **Air Dive** | Shift/Action (in air) | Extends horizontal momentum, allowing for long-distance gaps. |
| **Jump Master** | Passive | Increases overall jump height and reduces fall stun. |

This skill is a game-changer for exploration in [Pilgrammed](https://www.roblox.com/games/6713002033/Pilgrammed-BETA), allowing you to reach areas that were previously inaccessible without specific consumable items.

## Additional Prejunks Movement Skills

While the **pilgrammed great red feather** is required for the Jump Master skill, the 2026 Prejunks update introduced two other essential skills that complement it perfectly.

### Climbing Claws (Climb Master)
Talk to the **Duke of Rocks** in the Mountain region. You will need to complete a short quest involving defeating mountain enemies and reaching the peak. Once completed, he grants you the Climb Master skill. This allows you to climb walls up to three times consecutively before needing to touch the ground. When combined with the Jump Master's double jump, your vertical mobility becomes nearly limitless.

### Dash Technique (Dash Master)
Visit the **Elite Barracks** and speak with **Garth**. The exact requirements for this can vary, but generally, having a high level in the Elite Knight Guild or defeating the Skeleton boss will unlock the dialogue. The Dash Master skill replaces your standard roll with an empowered dash, providing more distance and faster recovery frames.

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Jump Master** | Adagio | Cloud City | Mid-air double jump and dive. |
| **Climb Master** | Duke of Rocks | Mountain | Triple wall climb. |
| **Dash Master** | Garth | Elite Barracks | High-speed dash/roll replacement. |

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the Great Red Bird located?**

A: The bird is located on the highest point of the farthest island in the Cloud City biome. You must use the white orb sequence or a Noxious Mortar to reach the summit.

**Q: Do I lose my Cloud Boots when I get the Jump Master skill?**

A: Yes, the Cloud Boots are consumed as part of the quest requirement to "upgrade" your movement into a permanent skill. However, the benefits of the boots are essentially integrated into your character's passive abilities.

**Q: Can I get the pilgrammed great red feather more than once?**

A: You can pluck the feather again if you lose it or if you are helping a friend, but since the Jump Master skill is a permanent unlock, you only need to successfully deliver it to Adagio once.

**Q: What happens if the Tendrilings knock me off during the orb sequence?**

A: If you are hit while gliding, you will lose your momentum and likely fall. It is highly recommended to have a high-health build or use items like the Sport Shawl to prevent being easily staggered during the flight to the **pilgrammed great red feather**.
