Navigating the vast and dangerous world of the popular Roblox RPG requires more than just high damage stats; it requires elite mobility. One of the most sought-after items in the current meta is the **pilgrammed redfeather**, officially known as the Great Redbird Feather. This rare crafting material is the key to unlocking the Air Jump skill, a game-changing ability that allows players to traverse difficult terrain and dodge boss mechanics with ease. Obtaining the **pilgrammed redfeather** is no simple task, as it involves a perilous journey across floating islands and a stealthy encounter with a giant avian creature.

In this guide, we will break down the exact steps required to find the Great Redbird Feather, the additional materials you need to collect, and how to navigate the challenging platforming sections of the Prejunks update. ## The Importance of the Great Redbird Feather

The primary reason players hunt for the **pilgrammed redfeather** is to unlock the Air Jump (or Double Jump) skill. In a game where positioning can mean the difference between victory and a long walk back from the graveyard, having a mid-air jump is invaluable. This skill doesn't just provide height; it also allows you to extend your dive, effectively mimicking the buffs provided by Scurry Boots but as a permanent, slot-free ability.

To begin this journey, you must first interact with the Skill Trainer NPC—specifically the one associated with the slingshot and mobility techniques. He won't simply give you the skill; he requires a set of rare components to "teach" your character the advanced movement.

### Required Materials for the Air Jump Skill

Before you set out to find the **pilgrammed redfeather**, ensure you have the other necessary components in your inventory. Farming these beforehand will save you multiple trips back to the NPC.

| Item | Quantity | Source/Location |
|---|---|---|
| **Cloud Boots** | 1 | Purchased from the Shop Vendor |
| **Royal Rubber** | 20 | Dropped by Imperial enemies or found in chests |
| **Great Redbird Feather** | 1 | Stolen from the Sleeping Redbird on the Far Island |

> **💡 Tip:** Always carry a Glider and ensure you have unlocked the Wall Climb skill before attempting this quest, as the platforming sections require high-level verticality.

## Step-by-Step Guide to the Pilgrammed Redfeather

The journey to find the **pilgrammed redfeather** begins at the Imperial Knight island. This area serves as the gateway to the more remote floating islands where the Great Redbird resides.

### 1. Reaching the Far Island
There are several ways to reach the distant islands, but the most efficient method involves using a teleporter. If you have the Noxious Mortar, you can use its explosive force to launch yourself toward the smaller islands. However, for most players, the standard path is as follows:
1. Travel to the island inhabited by the Imperial Knights.
2. Locate the smaller sub-island nearby and glide toward it.
3. Search the lower cliffs of this island to find a hidden teleporter.
4. Step into the teleporter to be transported to the "Far Island" chain.

### 2. Navigating the White Orbs
Once you arrive at the Far Island, you will notice glowing white orbs floating in the air. These are essential for your ascent. To use them, you must equip your Glider and fly directly over the orb. The orb will provide a significant lift and refresh your glide duration.

The sequence is tight, and timing is critical. You will need to chain several orbs together:
*   **Orb 1 to Orb 2:** A short jump that introduces the mechanic.
*   **The Vertical Wall:** After the second orb, you must use your Climbing Claws (Wall Climb skill) to scale a large vertical surface.
*   **The Final Stretch:** The last orb leads to a high-altitude platform where the environment becomes hostile.

### 3. Dealing with Tangerines
As you approach the summit, enemies known as "Tangerines" will begin to spawn. These orange, spherical threats are notorious for interrupting player movement. Because you cannot parry while gliding or climbing in this specific sequence, you must rely on your dodging skills.

> **Warning:** Getting hit by a Tangerine while in mid-air will cause hit-stun, likely leading to a fatal fall. Keep your camera focused on incoming projectiles and dodge early.

## Claiming the Redfeather
At the very top of the island chain, you will find the Great Redbird sleeping soundly. Unlike other bosses in *Pilgrammed*, you do not need to slay this creature to get the **pilgrammed redfeather**. In fact, attempting to fight it without the proper gear is a quick way to end your run.

Approach the sleeping bird carefully and interact with it to "Steal Feather." Once the item is in your inventory, you can safely descend or reset to return to the mainland. 

## Unlocking the Mobility Skill Tree
While the **pilgrammed redfeather** is the star of the show for many, it is part of a larger skill system introduced in the Prejunks update. To maximize your character's potential, you should aim to collect all three primary movement techniques.

| Skill Name | Requirement | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Wall Climb** | Duke of Rocks Quest | Allows 3 consecutive wall jumps |
| **Empowered Dash** | Elite Barracks (Garth) | Faster, longer-reaching dodge rolls |
| **Air Jump** | Redfeather + Materials | Mid-air jump and extended dive |

### The Wall Climb Skill
Before you can even think about the **pilgrammed redfeather**, you should obtain the Wall Climb skill from the Duke of Rocks. This NPC is located in the mountain region. You must complete his quest, which involves defeating a specific number of mountain-dwelling enemies and reaching the peak of the range. This skill acts as a permanent version of the Climbing Claws, allowing you to scale almost any surface in the game.

### The Empowered Dash
Located at the Elite Barracks, the NPC Garth (or Gareth) provides the Dash technique. While the exact unlock requirements can be vague, it is generally accepted that players need a high level within the Elite Knight Guild or must have defeated the Skeleton boss. This skill replaces your standard roll with a high-velocity dash, providing better I-frames and distance.

## Advanced Movement Tips
Once you have used your **pilgrammed redfeather** to unlock the Air Jump, you can combine these skills for incredible mobility. For example, you can Dash off a cliff, use your Air Jump to gain height, Glide to a wall, and then use your 3 Wall Clamber charges to reach previously inaccessible areas.

This combination is particularly useful in the late-game dungeons found on the [official Roblox Pilgrammed page](https://www.roblox.com/games/11135732551/Pilgrammed-BETA), where environmental hazards are just as deadly as the bosses themselves.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues
Many players struggle with the **pilgrammed redfeather** quest due to the platforming difficulty. If you find yourself falling repeatedly, consider the following:
*   **Weight Management:** Ensure your equipment weight isn't in the "Heavy" category, as this significantly reduces your jump height and glide distance.
*   **Jump Buffs:** Use items or consumables that provide a temporary jump boost before entering the teleporter.
*   **Camera Angle:** When using the white orbs, look slightly upward to ensure the boost sends you in the right direction.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get the pilgrammed redfeather more than once?**

A: Yes, you can collect multiple feathers if you wish to help friends or if future crafting recipes require them, but you only need one to unlock the Air Jump skill for your character.

**Q: What happens if the Great Redbird wakes up?**

A: If you trigger an attack or stay too long near the bird, it may wake up. It has a high health pool and powerful AOE attacks. It is generally recommended to grab the feather and leave immediately.

**Q: Do I need to be a certain level to use the redfeather?**

A: While there isn't a strict level requirement for the item itself, the enemies you encounter on the way (Imperial Knights and Tangerines) are balanced for mid-to-late game players. Being at least level 30-40 is recommended for a smooth run.

**Q: Can I trade the Great Redbird Feather to other players?**

A: As of the 2026 updates, most quest-specific materials like the **pilgrammed redfeather** are bound to your account or have specific trading restrictions to ensure players complete the platforming challenges themselves.
