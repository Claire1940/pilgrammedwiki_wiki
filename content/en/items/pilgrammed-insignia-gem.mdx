## Troubleshooting Your Build

If you find that your **pilgrammed insignia gem** setup isn't performing as expected, the issue usually lies in "stat diminishing returns." In 2026, stacking too much of a single stat (like Attack Speed) can lead to a cap where additional gems provide very little benefit. 

To avoid this, aim for a balanced distribution. If you have the King's Insignia equipped, you already have a strong foundation. Use your gem slots to fill in the gaps. For example, if your weapon is slow, use Topaz gems. If you find yourself dying too quickly, swap one offensive gem for a Diamond. The flexibility of the **pilgrammed insignia gem** system is its greatest strength, allowing you to adapt to new challenges as the developers release more content throughout the year.
