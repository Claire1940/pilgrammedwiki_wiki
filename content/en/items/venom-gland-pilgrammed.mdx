### Weapon Forms and Gimmicks
The Grave Tree features a unique "Two Form" system that rewards players for landing heavy attacks.

1.  **Normal Form:** Standard sideswipe M1 attacks. The heavy attack triggers a "Venom Rain" from the leaves, hitting four times.
2.  **Venom Form:** Triggered for 9 seconds after a successful Normal Form heavy attack. You will see purple venom particles lingering around the tree.
3.  **Venom Form Attacks:** Every M1 swing spits out three venom projectiles. The heavy attack is replaced by a ground slam that summons damaging rocks.

## Best Venom Builds for 2026

Creating a build around the **venom gland pilgrammed** materials requires balancing your critical hit chance with status effect duration. There are two primary ways to play:

## Advanced Combat Tactics

Using venom weapons effectively in Pilgrammed requires more than just button mashing. Because weapons like the Grave Tree have such high knockback (+140%), you can often keep enemies at a distance while the venom ticks away their health.

1.  **The Venom Rain Setup:** Always start a fight with your heavy attack. Even if you only land two of the four hits, the transformation into Venom Form is vital for your DPS.
2.  **Projectile Spacing:** In Venom Form, your M1s become semi-ranged. Use this to "kite" bosses like Old Fart or Kaiado. You don't need to be in their face to deal damage.
3.  **Element Synergy:** Since the Grave Tree deals Earth damage alongside Venom, it is highly effective against enemies weak to blunt force or nature-based attacks.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the best place to farm a venom gland pilgrammed?**

A: The best farming spot is the Spider Cave or the outskirts of the Grave Tree forest. Look for enemies with purple glowing accents, as these have the highest drop rate for venomous materials.

**Q: Does the Grave Tree's Venom Form last forever?**

A: No, the Venom Form lasts for approximately 9 seconds. You must land another heavy attack in Normal Form to refresh the duration. Look for the purple particles at the top of the tree to know when it is active.

**Q: Which accessory is best for a venom build?**

A: The **Caustic Fang** is widely considered the best accessory. It significantly boosts the damage and effectiveness of all venom-based attacks, making it a mandatory pick for anyone using the Grave Tree or Rot weapons.

**Q: Is the Grave Tree better than the Coffin Hilt?**

A: Both weapons have fun gimmicks. The Coffin Hilt is generally more versatile, but the Grave Tree offers higher burst potential and better crowd control thanks to its 140% knockback and projectile-spitting M1s in Venom Form.
