| Version | Rally Effect | Duration |
|---|---|---|

> **💡 Tip:** Since Rally now provides 26 HP over 2 seconds instead of a flat 20, it is technically more healing total, but much slower. Time your parries to ensure the regeneration isn't interrupted by subsequent hits.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the best place to farm pilgrammed gelatinous sludge?**

A: The best location is currently the Forgotten Village or the Catacombs. While the enemies are tougher, the drop rates for **pilgrammed gelatinous sludge** are significantly higher compared to the starting forest slimes.

**Q: Is the Slime Hammer better than the Cleric Tome?**

A: It depends on your build. The Slime Hammer is a physical, armor-piercing weapon ideal for melee builds. The Cleric Tome is better suited for support or magic-focused players. For solo progression against high-armor bosses, the Slime Hammer is generally preferred.

**Q: How do I get to the Forgotten Village?**

A: You must progress through the main story until you defeat the "Old Fart" boss. Once defeated, the path to the village opens up, allowing you to access the high-tier blacksmith and slime-related crafting recipes.

**Q: Did the Rally nerf make the Slime Hammer useless?**

A: Not at all. While the Rally nerf changed the timing of heals, the Slime Hammer's high penetration and heavy attack damage remain top-tier. You simply need to be more intentional with your parries to maximize the new 2-second healing window.
