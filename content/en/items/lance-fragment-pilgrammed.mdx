## Step 1: Crafting the Gemstone Pickaxe

You cannot simply walk into the Prism Troll’s lair. The entrance is sealed behind a specific environmental puzzle that requires the Gemstone Pickaxe. This tool is a prerequisite for any player hunting the **lance fragment pilgrammed** drops.

To obtain the Gemstone Pickaxe, head to the Savannah mirror and locate the blacksmith. He sells the recipe, but you will need to gather several rare ores from across the map first.

### Required Materials for Gemstone Pickaxe

| Material | Quantity | Location | Mining Tip |
| :--- | :---: | :--- | :--- |
| **Iron Pickaxe** | 1 | Blacksmith | Basic requirement for the upgrade. |
| **Ruby** | 5 | Volcano | Look for red-tinted ores scattered around the lava. |
| **Sapphire** | 5 | Mountain | Search for dark blue ores on the snowy peaks. |
| **Emerald** | 5 | Desert | Hunt for green ores amidst the sand dunes. |

Mining these can take some time as the gem drops are RNG-dependent. Once you have all 15 gems and your Iron Pickaxe, return to the Savannah blacksmith to finalize the tool.

## Step 2: Finding the Secret Prism Cave

With your Gemstone Pickaxe in hand, teleport to the Prairie. Your destination is the Thief King's Cave. This area is familiar to most early-game players, but the secret path to the Prism Troll is easily missed.

1.  Enter the Thief King’s Cave and proceed straight into the first large chamber.
2.  Look to the right side of the room for a set of stone steps.
3.  Climb to the very highest step.
4.  Equip your Gemstone Pickaxe and mine the floor of the highest step.
5.  The floor will break away, revealing a massive drop into a dark cavern.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The fall into the Prism Troll's lair is deep enough to kill most players. Ensure you have a glider equipped or use a move that negates fall damage before you make the jump.

## Step 3: Defeating the Prism Troll

The Prism Troll is widely regarded as the most difficult 1v1 boss in *Pilgrammed* as of 2026. He is fast, hits incredibly hard, and requires precise parrying to overcome. Unlike other bosses where you can simply out-range them, the Prism Troll's massive hitbox and speed mean you must stand your ground.

### Phase 1: The Rhythm of the Punch
The Troll primarily uses a three-punch combo. These are his most "readable" attacks. Your goal is to parry all three hits. After the combo, he will often follow up with a single, heavy punch that deals significant knockback. 

If you successfully parry the heavy punch, it goes on a 10-second cooldown. This is your window to deal guaranteed damage. While he is running at you, do not try to flee; his reach is too long. Stay calm, watch his arms, and time your blocks.

### Phase 2: The Laser Frenzy
Once the boss reaches 600 Health, he enters Phase 2. He becomes significantly faster and gains a laser beam attack.
*   **Laser Attack:** He fires three consecutive beams. These can be parried, but it is often safer to keep moving while timing your parries.
*   **Crystals:** Throughout the fight, he will summon crystals. Do not attempt to parry these; simply strafe left or right to avoid the projectiles.

If you want a guaranteed drop, you should attempt to fight the "Mondo" version of the boss. Killing a Mondo Prism Troll ensures a weapon drop, though the fight is significantly more difficult due to increased health and damage scaling.

## Recommended Builds for the Prism Lance

Because the Prism Lance scales well and provides utility, it fits into several 2026 meta builds.

### The Speed Scout Build
This build focuses on stacking the lance's speed buff with other agility-based accessories. By the time you reach five stacks, you will be moving faster than most mounts in the game, making you nearly impossible to hit in PvP scenarios.

### The Elemental Hybrid
Since the Prism Staff (the lance's sister drop) deals bleed and fire, many players run a hybrid setup. You use the Prism Lance for positioning and the Prism Staff for applying damage-over-time (DoT) effects to tanky bosses.

## Expert Combat Tips
*   **Don't Panic Roll:** The Prism Troll punishes players who spam dodge. His attacks have lingering hitboxes that catch you at the end of a roll.
*   **Parry Cooldowns:** Always keep track of the heavy punch cooldown. If you haven't seen the knockback punch in 10 seconds, expect it immediately after his next three-hit combo.
*   **Glider Utility:** If you get knocked back into the air by his crystals, deploy your glider immediately to reposition and avoid being caught in a juggle.

## FAQ

**Q: Where do I find the lance fragment pilgrammed players use for crafting?**

A: In the current version of the game, the "fragment" refers to the Prism Lance drop from the Prism Troll boss located in the secret cave under the Thief King's lair. There is no separate "fragment" item required for crafting the base version; it is a direct drop.

**Q: Can I mine the secret entrance with a regular Iron Pickaxe?**

A: No. The stone step in the Thief King's cave is reinforced. You specifically need the Gemstone Pickaxe, which requires Rubies, Sapphires, and Emeralds to craft.

**Q: What is the best way to dodge the Prism Troll's laser?**

A: The laser can be parried, which is the most effective way to stay in the fight. However, if your timing is off, sprinting in a tight circle around the boss is usually enough to avoid the beam's tracking.

**Q: Does the Prism Lance work with Strength or Dexterity builds?**

A: The Prism Lance is versatile and scales effectively with both, but it shines most in Dexterity-leaning builds due to the synergy between attack speed and the stacking movement speed buff.
