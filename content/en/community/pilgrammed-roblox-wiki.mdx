## Character Progression and Skill Points

One of the most important aspects of the **pilgrammed roblox wiki** is the documentation of stat scaling. Every time you level up, you receive skill points that can be distributed into various attributes. Understanding how these stats affect your gameplay is crucial for late-game viability.

### Primary Attributes

1.  **Strength:** Increases raw physical damage and allows for the use of heavier weaponry.
2.  **Dexterity:** Improves attack speed and certain mobility-based skills.
3.  **Intelligence:** Essential for magic-based builds and mana regeneration.
4.  **Vitality:** Increases your total health pool, which is mandatory for surviving boss "one-shot" mechanics.

## Navigating the Mid-Game: The Desert and Beyond

Once you leave the starting forest, the difficulty curve spikes significantly. The desert is home to enemies with more complex attack patterns and environmental hazards. To progress here, you must master the parry and dodge mechanics.

### Key Landmarks in the Desert
*   **The Oasis:** A safe zone where you can set your spawn point and find basic vendors.
*   **The Sandstone Ruins:** A high-risk area containing valuable loot and elite mob spawns.
*   **The Hidden Merchant:** An NPC that appears at specific times, selling rare upgrade materials.

Many players search the **pilgrammed roblox wiki** specifically for map coordinates or visual landmarks because the desert's vastness can be disorienting. Without a mini-map, players must rely on geological features to find their way.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The desert heat can drain your stamina faster if you are wearing heavy metal armor. Consider switching to lighter gear or finding "Cooling Charms" before long expeditions.

## Combat Mastery: Parrying and Dodging

In Pilgrammed, you cannot simply "M1" your way to victory against bosses. The combat system rewards precision.

*   **Parrying:** Pressing the block button just before an attack hits will negate all damage and often stun the attacker.
*   **Dodging:** Using your dash to move through attacks provides "i-frames" (invincibility frames), allowing you to reposition behind an enemy.

The **pilgrammed roblox wiki** contains detailed frame-data for these actions, which is invaluable for players aiming to take on "No-Hit" challenges or hardcore boss runs. Mastery of these mechanics is what separates a casual player from an expert.
