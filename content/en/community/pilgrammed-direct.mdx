## Essential Mobility Skills and Techniques

Mobility has received a massive overhaul in the latest update. Players can now unlock permanent upgrades that replace the need for specific equipment slots, allowing for more flexible builds. These skills are often tied to specific NPCs and require completing unique challenges or gathering rare materials.

### The Wall Climbing Technique
To unlock the ability to scale vertical surfaces more effectively, you must seek out the **Duke of Rocks**. Located in the mountainous region, this NPC provides a quest that requires you to clear out a specific number of mountain-dwelling enemies. Once the quest is completed and you reach the summit, the Duke grants you a permanent skill.

| Skill Name | Requirement | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Triple Wall Climb** | Complete Duke of Rocks' Mountain Quest | Allows 3 consecutive wall jumps before needing to touch the ground. |
| **Empowered Dash** | Talk to Gareth at Elite Barracks (Level/Guild req) | Replaces standard rolls with a faster, longer-distance dash. |
| **Cloud Jump** | 20 Royal Rubber, Redbird Feather, Cloud Boots | Enables a mid-air double jump and dive extension. |

### Unlocking the Double Jump
The most coveted skill in the **pilgrammed direct** update is the Cloud Jump (Double Jump). This requires a multi-stage scavenger hunt. First, purchase Cloud Boots from the standard shop vendor. Next, gather 20 Royal Rubber and the Great Redbird Feather. 

> **💡 Tip:** To reach the Redbird Island, use a Noxious Mortar for a vertical boost or take the teleporter located near the Imperial Knights' island.

Once on the island, you must navigate a series of white orbs using a glider. These orbs refresh your flight duration. Avoid the aggressive Tangerine enemies that spawn during the flight sequence; since you cannot parry while gliding, focus entirely on dodging and reaching the next orb quickly. At the end of the path, interact with the sleeping Redbird to secure the feather.

## New Elemental Wands and Ranged Weaponry

Magic and ranged combat have seen a resurgence with the introduction of specialized wands. These weapons often feature unique heavy attacks that can synergize with specific armor sets, such as the Sunbreaker set.

### Volcanic and Mountainous Wands
The **Demo Wand** is a standout for high-intensity builds. It features a rapid-fire primary attack with low mana consumption. However, its heavy attack causes an explosion that deals damage to the user. While this sounds detrimental, it is incredibly effective for triggering "on-damage" buffs from high-end armor sets.

| Weapon | Location | Materials Needed | Unique Property |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Demo Wand** | Volcano NPC | 200 Gold, 8 Demo, 1 Fire Stone | Self-damaging explosion on heavy attack. |
| **Mythro Wand** | Mountain Region | Mythro Gold | Fires homing icicles and lasers. |
| **Magma Shotgun** | Volcano Shop | 500 Gold, Blunderbuss, Ruby, 10 Magma Scales | High-damage spread that applies Burn. |
| **Vine Wand** | Swamp (Friendly Witch) | Crafting Materials | Tracks enemies with venomous vines. |

The **Vine Wand**, found in the swamp region, introduces a "Grass" element. This weapon is unique because it cannot be fired into empty space; it requires a nearby target to manifest vines from the ground. Its heavy attack is particularly useful for crowd control, as it roots enemies in place.

## Boss Drops and Legendary Melee Weapons

For those who prefer close-quarters combat, the **pilgrammed direct** update includes several high-tier drops from world bosses and hidden chests. The Sinister Sea is currently the most lucrative area for these items.

### Sinister Sea Loot
Defeating the **Big Fish** boss grants access to the Big Fish Gauntlet. Despite its appearance, this is classified as a Mage weapon. It fires electric projectiles that bounce between multiple enemies, making it ideal for clearing rooms of smaller mobs.

Conversely, the **Bone Claws** (dropped by Patra) are a top-tier Fist weapon. They feature a unique passive called "Bloodthirst." When active, the user gains significantly increased attack range and damage output. This weapon is highly recommended for players who enjoy high-risk, high-reward aggressive playstyles.

### The Black Steel Dagger Upgrade
The Pitch Dagger is a solid mid-game choice found at Fang's shop in the swamp, but it can be evolved. By taking the Pitch Dagger to the Blackbird NPC located in the Chasm of the Volcano, players can trade it for the **Black Steel**. This version retains the dash-on-hit mechanic but features significantly higher base stats and a sleek aesthetic.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Chasm is a high-hazard zone. Use the Noxious Mortar or precise platforming to reach the Blackbird without falling into the lava.

## Secret Catacombs Weapon: The Morph Spell

One of the more complex weapons to find is "The Morph Spell," hidden deep within the Catacombs. Unlike standard shop items, this weapon must be scavenged from the environment. Navigate to the Jailer area and search the various tombs scattered throughout the side rooms.

The Morph Spell is a versatile tool for mana-heavy builds. It features a cycling spell system:
1. **Light Status:** Changes the enemy's elemental affinity to Light, making them vulnerable to specific counter-elements.
2. **Cursed Fire:** A self-buff that applies fire but carries a risk.
3. **Shadow Summons:** Spawns shadow-clones of catacomb enemies to fight alongside you. There is no limit to the number of summons other than your total mana pool.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get the Great Redbird Feather for the double jump?**

A: You must travel to the far island via the Imperial Knight teleporter, use a glider to navigate a series of white orbs that refresh your flight, and interact with the sleeping Redbird at the end of the course.

**Q: Where is the best place to farm Royal Rubber for pilgrammed direct crafting?**

A: Royal Rubber is most commonly found as a drop from enemies in the Imperial areas or purchased from specific high-level wandering merchants.

**Q: Is the Magma Shotgun better than the standard Blunderbuss?**

A: Yes, the Magma Shotgun is a direct upgrade, offering higher base damage and the ability to apply a burn status effect, though it requires a Ruby and Magma Scales to craft.

**Q: Can I unlock the Dash technique early in the game?**

A: The Dash technique requires talking to Gareth at the Elite Barracks. While the exact requirements can vary, having a high level in the Elite Knight guild or defeating the Skeleton boss is generally required to unlock the dialogue.
