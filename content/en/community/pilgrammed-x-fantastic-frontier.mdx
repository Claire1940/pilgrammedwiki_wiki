## Finding the Pitfalls: Glowester’s Locations

To begin your journey into the **pilgrammed x fantastic frontier** crossover, you must first locate the new NPC named Glowester. Found in most major villages, Glowester acts as your guide to the "Pitfalls"—interdimensional rifts that lead to the collab content. While he won't tell you exactly when a rift is active, he provides the general locations where they can spawn.

There are six primary locations where these rifts appear. Finding them is the only way to access the Road Dimension and eventually the boss.

| Location | Specific Landmark | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Prairie** | Waterfall (Right Side) | Located right in the middle of the upper waterfall area. |
| **The Mountain** | Big Bay Overlook | High up next to a lone tree overlooking the bay. |
| **The Swamp** | Mirror Waterfall | Situated on the cliffside near the mirror portal. |
| **Desert Ruins** | Big Iron Cliff | On the high ridges overlooking the industrial ruins. |
| **Cloud Wilds** | Sunshot Exit | Spawns immediately after exiting the Sunshot path. |
| **The Forest** | Stone Platform | Located near Palm on a raised stone structure. |

> **💡 Tip:** Glowester's dialogue changes based on which rift is currently active. Always check with him in a village before heading out to save travel time.

## The Demo Can: Boss Guide and Mechanics

The centerpiece of the **pilgrammed x fantastic frontier** update is the Demo Can. This boss is notorious for its high health pool and punishing attack strings. It boasts a base health of 10,000, which scales by an additional 5,000 HP for every extra player in the session. With 10 defense and 80% damage reduction, you cannot simply brute-force this encounter without a proper build.

### Attack Patterns
The Demo Can utilizes a mix of parryable sword strikes and unparryable "gimmick" attacks. You can identify unparryable moves by a distinct **red flash** that occurs just before the animation begins.

| Attack Name | Type | Effect/Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Double Swing** | Parryable | A slow initial swing followed by a lightning-fast jab. |
| **Heavy Slam** | Parryable | A single, heavy downward strike with a long wind-up. |
| **Make Flame** | Parryable | Slams sword to create puddles of fire in a small radius. |
| **Charge Attack** | Unparryable | Drags swords to create two parallel lines of fire. |
| **Hell** | Debuff | Spins and screams, applying **Deafened** (-50% healing/mana regen). |
| **Flames of Annihilation** | Phase 2 | A continuous torrent of fire. **Hide behind rocks** to survive. |

During the "Hell" phase, the boss will also drop missile-like objects that spawn two types of adds:
*   **Spikers:** Fast-moving, low-health enemies that deal 250 damage.
*   **Boomers:** Slow, high-health tanks that deal a massive 500 damage upon contact.

For players struggling with survival, a high-sustain build like the **Nos Druid** is recommended. This setup allows you to tank hits while utilizing Cursed Fire, which scales with the boss's maximum health to deal consistent tick damage.

## The Mystery Store and Manager Supreme

Defeating the Demo Can for the first time grants you a guaranteed **Member's Key Card**. This is a permanent item that grants access to the Mystery Store, located on a secluded cliff within the Forest. Inside, you will find the Manager Supreme, a merchant who sells the most powerful items in the **pilgrammed x fantastic frontier** collection.

The store is the primary source for the Red Power Fists, Rat Boy Handgun, and other high-tier equipment. While some of these items can be found as rare drops in other areas (like the Otherworld Tower or Rising Waters), the Mystery Store is the only reliable way to complete your collection.

## New Weapons: Stats and Gimmicks

The 2026 update introduces five weapons, each with complex scaling and unique techniques that set them apart from standard Pilgrammed gear.

### 1. Red Power Fists (Gauntlet)
A heavy metal melee weapon that rewards aggressive play. It cannot be used with glove accessories, but its built-in combo system makes up for the lack of equipment slots.
*   **Damage:** 30 (Metal Element)
*   **Gimmick:** A 5-hit combo that culminates in two massive strikes.
*   **Heavy Attack:** A charged "Echo Punch" that reverberates, hitting enemies multiple times.

### 2. Rat Boy Handgun (Pistol)
Arguably the best ranged weapon in the current meta. It functions similarly to the Six Shooter but includes a "Divine Shot" technique.
*   **Damage:** 45 (Divine)
*   **Divine Shot:** Pierces defense. Instantly disintegrates non-boss enemies below 10% health. Reduces boss damage/defense by 50% if they are below 20% health.
*   **Passive:** The final bullet in the magazine triggers a divine burst for extra damage and knockback.

### 3. Jade Twin (Hybrid Blade)
A mystical blade that switches between two distinct fighting styles. It is a favorite for hybrid builds that balance Dexterity and Intellect.
*   **One-Handed Stance:** Scales with **Dexterity**. Focuses on rapid, low-lag slashes.
*   **Two-Handed Stance:** Scales with **Intellect**. Consumes mana to fire waves of light.
*   **Technique:** Performing a stance swap immediately after a heavy attack triggers a unique offensive morph slash.

### 4. Wicked Junk Spell Book (Magic)
A high-speed magic weapon that excels at sustained DPS. It is widely considered the strongest non-instakill mage weapon in the **pilgrammed x fantastic frontier** update.
*   **Damage:** 40 (Metal Element)
*   **Technique:** Grants 50% decreased startup and cooldown for 10-12 seconds.
*   **Gimmick:** Summons "Ring Toasters" that inflict fire damage and mimic your M1 attacks.

### 5. Funky Drift Lander (Great Spear)
A bizarre spear with massive range and a high-skill ceiling.
*   **Damage:** 35 (Dark Element)
*   **Range:** 12 (Extraordinarily long)
*   **Drift Technique:** The player "drifts" across the battlefield. The longer the drift is maintained, the larger the explosion upon hitting an enemy.

> **Warning:** The Funky Drift Lander has significant end-lag if you miss your drift. Only use the technique when you have a clear opening.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get more Rat Tokens in the pilgrammed x fantastic frontier update?**

A: Rat Tokens are earned by defeating the Demo Can boss. Each successful kill rewards between 20 to 30 tokens, which can be spent at the Mystery Store for rare items and consumables.

**Q: Is the Member's Key Card a one-time use item?**

A: No, the Member's Key Card is a permanent unlock. Once you defeat the Demo Can and receive it, you can access Manager Supreme’s shop in the Forest whenever you like.

**Q: Which weapon is the best for a pure Intellect build?**

A: The Wicked Junk Spell Book is the top choice for pure mages due to its incredible fire rate and the "Ring Toaster" gimmick. However, the Jade Twin is a strong secondary option if you want a melee weapon that scales with Intellect.

**Q: Can I still get the collab items if I don't participate in the boss fight?**

A: While the Mystery Store is the easiest way to get these items, some weapons like the Wicked Junk Spell Book and Funky Drift Lander have a very small chance to drop from high-level activities like the Otherworld Tower or Rising Waters. However, the Rat Boy Handgun and Red Power Fists are much harder to find outside of the collab-specific areas.
