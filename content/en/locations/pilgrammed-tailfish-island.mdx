Navigating the vast and often treacherous seas of Roblox's hit RPG, Pilgrammed, requires a keen eye for detail and a sturdy ship. One of the most elusive locations in the current 2026 meta is **pilgrammed tailfish island**, a remote landmass nestled within the Savannah region. While it may appear desolate at first glance, **pilgrammed tailfish island** is a critical destination for players looking to unlock the "Old Fart" secret boss and obtain some of the most powerful late-game accessories and weapons available.

In this comprehensive guide, we will walk you through the exact steps to locate this island, the prerequisites needed to interact with its secrets, and a full strategy for the boss fight that follows. ## Navigating to Pilgrammed Tailfish Island

To begin your journey to **pilgrammed tailfish island**, you must first reach the Savannah region. This area is known for its golden sands and dangerous wildlife, but the real secrets lie off the coast.

1.  **Teleport to the Savannah Mirror:** Ensure you have the Savannah mirror unlocked for quick travel.
2.  **Spawn a Ship:** Head to the docks and summon your fastest vessel.
3.  **Set Sail:** From the Savannah docks, sail directly forward into the open water.
4.  **Identify the Island:** You are looking for a small, rocky island that appears mostly empty. This is **pilgrammed tailfish island**.

While the island doesn't feature sprawling cities or many NPCs, it houses a hidden wall that can only be breached with a specific tool. Before you set sail, you must ensure you have the **Gemstone Pickaxe** in your inventory.

| Tool | Required Materials | Mining Locations |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gemstone Pickaxe** | **Rubies** | Volcano |
| | **Emeralds** | Desert |
| | **Sapphires** | Mountain |

> **Warning:** Do not attempt to visit the island without the Gemstone Pickaxe if your goal is the secret boss. The mushroom engravings are immune to standard mining tools.

## The Mushroom Puzzle Quest

Visiting **pilgrammed tailfish island** is only one part of a three-stage puzzle required to open the door in the Condemned Passage. The "Old Fart" boss is locked behind a door engraved with mushrooms, and you must find three hidden engravings across the world to proceed.

### Mushroom 1: The Abandoned Lighthouse
Travel to the Forest mirror and head toward the Abandoned Lighthouse. On a small ledge near the building, you will spot a mushroom on the ground. Use your Gemstone Pickaxe to mine the wall directly behind it. Press **G** to interact with the engraving once it is revealed.

### Mushroom 2: Tailfish Island
This is where your trip to **pilgrammed tailfish island** comes into play. Once you arrive at the island in the Savannah, head around the corner of the main rock formation. You will find a similar mushroom on the floor. Mine the wall, interact with the engraving, and you will receive a cryptic message indicating the seal is weakening.

### Mushroom 3: The Cloud City Ledge
Take the slingshot at Cloud City and pass through the two teleporters. Head behind the large white building and look toward the horizon to see a distant island. Use your glider to reach the ledge at the back of this island. Mine the final wall to complete the puzzle.

## The Condemned Passage Parkour

Before you can use the progress made at **pilgrammed tailfish island**, you must navigate the Condemned Passage. This area is located in a hole in the ground between the Forest and the Desert.

The passage is notoriously dark and foggy. We recommend equipping a "Speed Demon" build or any agility-boosting gear to make the jumps easier. 

1.  **The Mirror:** Upon entering the first room, turn right and look for a wooden board propped against the wall.
2.  **The Fog:** Follow the path behind the board into a platforming section. Because of the limited visibility, it is easy to fall.
3.  **The Bank:** Always deposit your gold in the bank before entering, as deaths in the fog can result in significant currency loss.
4.  **The Drop:** At the end of the parkour, there is an opening in the floor. Jump down to find the boss room mirror.

## Boss Guide: Defeating the Old Fart

Once all three mushrooms (including the one from **pilgrammed tailfish island**) have been activated, the door in the Condemned Passage will open. Interacting with the central mushroom inside will teleport you to an open field to face the Old Fart.

### Phase 1: The Melee Duel
The boss will not attack until you strike first. Use this opportunity to setup buffs or land a heavy initial hit.
*   **Parrying:** This is a parry-heavy fight. Focus on his swing timings.
*   **Headbutt:** When he leans back for a headbutt, dodge sideways or time a perfect parry to stagger him.
*   **Rally Skill:** Keep the Rally skill equipped. Since his attacks are frequent, successful parries will keep your health topped off without needing potions.

### Phase 2: The Laser Barrage
At 50% health, the boss will throw ink on your screen, obscuring your vision, and begin using magical attacks.

| Attack Move | Survival Strategy |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Laser Pillars** | Constant movement; do not stand in the glowing circles on the ground. |
| **Rectangle Lasers** | These can be parried. Watch the trajectory and time your block. |
| **Floor Lasers** | Stay close to the boss at an angle. This forces him to use melee moves which are easier to manage than the area-of-effect floor hazards. |

> **Tip:** If the ink on the screen makes parrying difficult, zoom your camera out as far as possible to see the rectangular telegraphs more clearly.

## Rewards and Loot Drops

The effort of traveling to **pilgrammed tailfish island** and completing the parkour is well-rewarded. The Old Fart drops items that are essential for specific class builds.

*   **Spore Shot (50% Drop Rate):** A powerful gun that shoots projectiles which split into multiple smaller balls. Excellent for "proc" builds that rely on multiple hits to trigger explosion gems.
*   **Shrumerang (50% Drop Rate):** A unique melee weapon with ranged capabilities. The heavy attack throws three boomerangs at once. Note that it does not deal "return" damage if the initial hit connects.
*   **Mushroom Cap (35% Drop Rate):** A top-tier Mage helmet. It provides a massive **+40 Intellect** boost, though it significantly penalizes Agility and Dexterity.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is Pilgrammed Tailfish Island located?**

A: It is located in the Savannah sea. From the Savannah mirror/docks, sail straight forward. It is a small, relatively empty island that contains one of the three secret mushroom engravings.

**Q: Can I mine the mushroom wall with a regular pickaxe?**

A: No. You must use the Gemstone Pickaxe, which is crafted at the Savannah blacksmith using Rubies, Emeralds, and Sapphires.

**Q: What is the best build for fighting the Old Fart boss?**

A: A high-sustain melee build with the Rally skill is recommended for Phase 1. For Phase 2, having some ranged capability or high magic defense helps mitigate the damage from his laser pillars.

**Q: Does Tailfish Island have any other purpose?**

A: In addition to the boss quest, **pilgrammed tailfish island** is often used by players for high-level fishing, as the Tailfish NPC can sometimes be found in the vicinity or related Savannah waters.
