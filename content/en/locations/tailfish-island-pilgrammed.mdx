## Navigating to Tailfish Island Pilgrammed: The Journey Starts Here

To begin your quest, you must first reach the Savannah. This region is accessible early on, but the waters can be treacherous if you aren't prepared for naval combat. Once you arrive at the Savannah mirror, your first objective is to secure a vessel.

Tailfish Island is located directly forward from the Savannah mirror's dock. It is a small, sandy landmass that lacks NPCs or standard shops, which is why most players overlook it. However, if you are following the Old Fart boss questline, this island is where you will find the second of three required mushroom engravings.

| Location Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Primary Landmark** | Small rocky outcrop in the Savannah Sea |
| **Key Objective** | Hidden Mushroom Engraving behind a breakable wall |
| **Recommended Gear** | Gemstone Pickaxe (Required) |
| **Enemy Density** | Low/None |

> **💡 Tip:** Before heading to the island, ensure you have crafted the Gemstone Pickaxe. You cannot interact with the hidden engravings without it.

## The Condemned Passage and the Foggy Parkour

Before you can make use of the secrets found on **tailfish island pilgrammed**, you need to locate the boss's lair. The entrance is hidden in a hole in the ground located between the Forest and the Desert biomes. This area is known as the Condemned Passage.

Upon entering the hole, you will find a dark, foggy cavern. This area is notorious for its low visibility, which can lead to accidental deaths. It is highly recommended that you deposit your gold in a bank before attempting this section, as the parkour is unforgiving.

1.  **Locate the Wooden Board:** In the first room of the passage, turn right and look for a wooden board propped against the wall.
2.  **Navigate the Fog:** Follow the path behind the board into a parkour section. The darkness makes judging distances difficult, so take your time.
3.  **Activate the Mirror:** At the bottom of the parkour area, there is a mirror. Activate this immediately so you never have to perform the parkour again.
4.  **The Sealed Door:** Beyond the mirror is a massive stone door engraved with three mushroom symbols. This door will remain sealed until you find the corresponding engravings across the world.

## Crafting the Gemstone Pickaxe

The "Old Fart" questline requires a specific tool to "mine" the mushroom engravings. You can purchase the blueprint for the Gemstone Pickaxe from the Blacksmith located in the Savannah. This tool requires three different types of gems found in various biomes.

| Gem Type | Mining Location | Recommended Prep |
|---|---|---|
| **Ruby** | Volcano | Heat resistance gear/potions |
| **Emerald** | Desert | High mobility for sand dunes |
| **Sapphire** | Mountain | Cold resistance or quick movement |

Once you have gathered these materials, craft the pickaxe at any anvil. This tool is not just for this quest; it is a highly efficient mining tool for various late-game ores found throughout the [official Pilgrammed Roblox page](https://www.roblox.com/games/11327123382/Pilgrammed) experience.

## The Three Mushroom Locations

With your Gemstone Pickaxe in hand, you must visit three specific locations to unlock the door in the Condemned Passage.

### 1. The Abandoned Lighthouse (Forest)
Teleport to the Forest mirror and head toward the Abandoned Lighthouse. Look for a small ledge nearby. On the ground, you will see a small mushroom. Use your Gemstone Pickaxe to mine the wall directly behind it. When the engraving appears, press **G** to interact with it. You will see "edgy" text appear on your screen, confirming the progress.

### 2. Tailfish Island (Savannah)
As mentioned earlier, take your ship from the Savannah mirror and sail straight ahead. On **tailfish island pilgrammed**, search the corners of the rock formations. You will find a similar breakable wall. Mine it, interact with the engraving, and move to the final location.

### 3. The Sky Island (Cloud City)
This is the most difficult mushroom to reach. Take the slingshot to Cloud City and move through the two teleporters. Go behind the large white building and look into the distance to see a remote island. Use your glider to reach a small ledge on the back of this island. Mine the wall at the end of the ledge to claim the final mushroom.

## Boss Guide: How to Defeat the Old Fart

Once all three mushrooms are activated, return to the Condemned Passage. The door will now open, leading to an open field. The Old Fart boss will not attack you until you strike him first, allowing you to set up your buffs and get a "free" opening hit.

### Phase 1: The Melee Duel
In the first phase, the boss relies heavily on melee swings and a rectangular laser move.
*   **Parrying:** This is a parry-heavy fight. Almost all of his swings can be parried to regain health via the **Rally** skill.
*   **Headbutt:** Watch for his headbutt animation; it has a slightly different timing than his sword swings.
*   **Jump Attack:** When he leaps into the air, do not try to parry. Instead, dodge to the side to avoid the AOE impact.

### Phase 2: The Laser Barrage
At 50% health, the boss will throw ink on your screen to obscure your vision and begin using advanced laser moves.
*   **Laser Pillars:** Pillars of light will rain from the sky. Keep moving in a circular motion to avoid these.
*   **Rectangular Lasers:** He will fire two horizontal lasers. These can be parried if your timing is precise.
*   **Floor Lasers:** When he summons lasers on the ground, stay close to the boss. This forces him to use melee moves that you can parry, keeping you stationary and safe from the floor hazards.

> **Warning:** If you are hit by a melee attack while floor lasers are active, the knockback might send you into a laser beam, resulting in an instant combo-kill.

## Loot and Rewards Analysis

Defeating the Old Fart is highly rewarding, especially for players using specific builds. The drops focus on multi-hit capabilities and magic power.

| Item | Drop Rate | Stats & Utility |
|---|---|---|
| **Spore Shot** | 50% | A gun that fires projectiles which split into smaller balls. Excellent for proccing "Explosion" gems. |
| **Mushroom Cap** | 35% | A top-tier Mage helmet. Provides **+40 Intellect** and **+12 Defense**, but reduces Agility and Dexterity. |
| **Shrumerang** | 50% | A hybrid melee/ranged weapon. Its heavy attack throws three boomerangs at once. |

The **Shrumerang** is particularly interesting for melee users. While it does 30 base damage, the fact that it can hit enemies multiple times on its return path makes it a prime candidate for status-effect gems. However, note that a single boomerang cannot damage the same target twice in a single "flight" (it won't hit on the way out *and* the way back on the same frame).

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I see the mushroom engraving on Tailfish Island?**

A: You must have the Gemstone Pickaxe equipped and mine the specific section of the wall near the small floor mushroom. If it still doesn't appear, ensure you haven't already activated it by checking the door in the Condemned Passage.

**Q: Is the Mushroom Cap good for all classes?**

A: No, the Mushroom Cap is specifically designed for Mages. While the +40 Intellect is massive, the -30 Agility and -20 Dexterity penalties will severely hurt melee and ranged builds that rely on movement and attack speed.

**Q: How do I get back to the boss if I die?**

A: If you activated the mirror at the bottom of the foggy parkour section in the Condemned Passage, you can simply teleport back there using any other mirror in the world. You do not need to repeat the parkour or the mushroom hunt.

**Q: What is the best build for fighting the Old Fart?**

A: A high-sustain melee build using the Speed Demon set or any build with the Rally skill is highly effective. Since the boss has many parryable attacks, being able to heal through parries makes the fight much more manageable. Use the secrets of **tailfish island pilgrammed** to your advantage by coming prepared with high-intellect spells if you prefer a ranged approach.
